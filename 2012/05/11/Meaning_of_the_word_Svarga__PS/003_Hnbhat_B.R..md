*यन्न दुःखेन संभिन्नं*न च ग्रस्तमनन्तरम्।

अभिलाषोपनीतं च तत्सुखं स्वःपदास्पदम्\|\|

  

"स्वरव्ययं स्वर्गनाक-" the Amara citation holds both as synonyms irrespective of their derivations, as the first is अव्यय while the second is declined in all the seven cases.

  

The other meanings are conventional and contextual meanings given in the Vedic vocabulary. Mostly independent of the context, as per the maximumउपक्रमोपसंहारौ अभ्यासो &पूर्वता फलम्।

अर्थवादोपपत्ती च लिङ्गं तात्पर्यनिर्णये laid by the commentators for the interpretation of Vedic Vocabulary which follows the dictum of Yaska "नैकं पदं निर्ब्रूयात्" no word should be interpreted out of its context followed by himself in his interpretation of the Vedic Vocabulary in 12 Chapters.
