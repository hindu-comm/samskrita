It is the same function-wise classification as applied to Paninian rules, already posted in the first post itself with specifying the function.

  

It is not only interesting, but it is necessary and essential to understand the functions of each type of these rules to derive word declination or conjugation or any inflectional form with the help of them. It is the very scheme of Paninian Grammar.
