hariH OM,  
  
kAtyAyanii is one of the epithets of durgaa / pArvatii.  
she is called so because she was born in the vaMsha of kata R^iShi during a different kalpa (according to the sanskrit kannaDa dictionary written by shriinivAsa gopAlAcArya).  
  
katasya gotrApatyaM strii. (i don't know vyAkaraNa, i may be totally wrong, i request vidvAns to correct me if i am wrong).  
  
svasti,  
 JAYA BHAVAANII BHAARATII,  

shrivathsa.
