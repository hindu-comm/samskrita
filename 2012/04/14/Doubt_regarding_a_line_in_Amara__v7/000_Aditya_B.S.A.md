नमो नमः

  

In the line 'soorasooryaaryamaadityadvaadashaatmadivaakaraah' (dig-varga 28th shloka) how have the consecutive terms 'dvaadashaatmaa' and 'divaakarah' come together as dvaadashaatmadivaakarah? How is there a hrasva a-kaara between the two? What sandhi has taken place?

  

Regards,

  

Aditya.
