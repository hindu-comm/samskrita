Scanned copies of the Śrīrāghavakṛpābhāṣyam commentaries by Jagadguru Rāmabhadrācārya on seven more Upaniṣads are now available for free reading and download from the Shri Tulsi Peeth Seva Nyas website. These bilingual commentaries in Sanskrit and Hindi were first published in 1998, and released by Sh. Atal Behari Vajpayee, the then Prime Minister of India.
