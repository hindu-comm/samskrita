Declension is as follows:

अगमत्-अगमताम्-अगमन्; अगमः-अगमतम्-अगमत; अगमम्-अगमाव-अगमाम

It is लुङ् of गम्.

When माङ् is used along with लुङ् it has the force of an imperative. अ of अगमः also gets lost.

There are other scholars in this group who can quote supporting Panini सूत्रs.
