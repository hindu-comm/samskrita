It is popular that Muni Patanjali, himself had written three works

  

1.  योगसूत्र - to purify mind, 
2.  महाभाष्य commentary on अष्टाध्ययी - in शब्दशास्त्र to purify the
    language. 3.  चरकसंहिता - compilation of the सिद्धान्त-s of of चरक school in
    आयुर्वेद, to preserve health of body, respectively.
