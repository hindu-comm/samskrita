The next rules to

  

अतिशयाने तमबिष्ठनौ।

  

cover these examples,

  

  

तिङश्च॥[५।३।५६](http://sanskritdocuments.org/learning_tools/sarvanisutrani/GoToSutram/5.3.56)\|\|

and

द्विवचनविभज्योपपदे तरबीयसुनौ॥[५।३।५७](http://sanskritdocuments.org/learning_tools/sarvanisutrani/GoToSutram/5.3.57)\|\|  

  

which prescribe the suffixes तरप् and तमप् for तिङन्त-s,

  

added with the sUtra:

  

किमेत्तिङव्ययघादाम्वद्रव्यप्रकर्षे॥[५।४।११](http://sanskritdocuments.org/learning_tools/sarvanisutrani/GoToSutram/5.4.11)॥  

  

gives you the required forms:

  

पचतितराम्। पचतितमाम्। विजयतेतराम् - विजयतेतमाम्॥  

  

as these two suffixes are designated as घ - as perसूत्रम्॥

तरप्तमपौ घः॥[१।१।२१](http://sanskritdocuments.org/learning_tools/sarvanisutrani/GoToSutram/1.1.21)॥  

  

I need not mention तिङ् includes both तङ् suffixes also in the examples given by you which is governed by theसूत्रम्॥विपराभ्यां जेः॥[१।३।१९](http://sanskritdocuments.org/learning_tools/sarvanisutrani/GoToSutram/1.3.19)\|\| .

  

Note the number may vary to different versions of Ashtadhyayi. The numbering and the hyperlinks are to the Ashtadhyayi in Sanskrit documents list. In the order of derivation,

  

"विपराभ्यां जेः" इति तङ् - वि+जयते।  

" द्वयोर्विभज्योपपदे तरबीयसुनौ"

"तिङश्च"  

इति तरप्तमौ। = घः

  

"किमेत्तिङ्ङव्ययघादाम्वद्रव्यप्रकर्षे" इति आम्। >\> विजयतेतर- विजयतेतम + आम् = विजयतेतराम्, विजयतेतमाम्॥
