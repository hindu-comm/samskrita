धातुपाठ is your first guide to decide the conjugation type it demands. But it is some times overridden by a specific rule deviating from its class due to meaning or the विकरण suffix. Best method is to learn the conjugations with the help of लघुसिद्धान्तकौमुदी, मध्यसिद्धान्तकौमुदी, सिद्धान्तकौमुदी - which are graded text books for the beginners to introduce them to the study intensive of the original source book Panini's अष्टाध्यायी.

  

काशिका-वृत्तिः  

वा भ्राश-भ्लाश-भ्रमु-क्रमु-क्लमु-त्रसि-त्रुति-लषः३।१।७०\|\|

उभयत्र विभाषेयम्। टुभ्राशृ, दुभ्लाशृ = दीप्तौ, भ्रमु = अनवस्थाने, भ्रमु = चलने, द्वयोरपि ग्रहणम्, क्रमु = पादविक्षेपे, क्लमु = ग्लानौ, त्रसी = उद्वेगे, त्रुटी = छेदने, लष = कान्तौ,

  

एतेभ्यो वा श्यन् प्रत्ययो भवति।

  

भ्राश्यते, भ्राशते। भ्लाश्यते, भ्लाशते। भ्राम्यति, भ्रमति। क्राम्यति, क्रामति। क्लाम्यति, क्लामति। त्रस्यति, त्रसति। त्र्युट्यति, त्रुटति। लष्यति, लषति।

  

एभ्यः श्यन्वा कर्त्रर्थे सार्वधातुके परे। पक्षे शप्॥  

  

There are certain others for which the rules deviate them with or without change like this. Though these are listed in दिवादि the 4th group of conjugation, due to which general lists give the form common to the group, it is interchangeable with the general suffix prescribed for भ्वादि due to the operation of this rule. A perfect list of conjugations should give both the alternative forms also.

  

Here is the list of rules prescribing suffixes based on the groups in the धातुपाठ with alternative prescriptions such as shone above:
