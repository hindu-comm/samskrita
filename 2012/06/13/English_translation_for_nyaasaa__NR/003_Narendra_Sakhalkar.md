Nyaasa means something given to someone else to be taken care of. until the owner comes back to take it.The quotation is from Mahakavi Bhasa's play, Swapnawasawadattam.\[Act I\].Padmawatee, the princess is about to take Wasawadatta in her custody, at the request of her Minister, Yougandharayana, disguised as a Sanyasee.
