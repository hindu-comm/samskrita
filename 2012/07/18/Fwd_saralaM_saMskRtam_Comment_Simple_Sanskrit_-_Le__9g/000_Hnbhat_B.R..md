वृन्दावनमहिमस्तोत्रम् - bySrila Prabodhananda Sarasvati Thakura

\|\|८.५२॥



1. जरीहर्तिं स्वान्तं हरि हरि युवत्यादिविषयो

2. नरीनर्त्याक्रम्य त्वतिशिरसि माया भगवतः ।

3. वरीवर्त्यत्याशा चिरमिह तु वृन्दावनवने

4. न जाने राधा वा कथमिव चरीकर्ति करुणाम्॥॥



52

  

1\. Alas! Alas! The forms of young women and other objects of sense gratification repeatedly steal away my heart.

  

2\. The illusory potency of the Supreme Personality of Godhead attacks me and repeatedly dances on my head.

  

3\. I continually aspire to remain always here in Vrndavana forest.

  

4\. Will Srimati Radharani be always merciful to me? I do not know.



The above is a verse using 4 frequentativeverbs consequently in 4 paada-s from the simple verbs हृ. नृत्, वृत्, and कृ. The translation is by Kusakratha Dasa.

  

I have made the modifications to the text as provided in sanskritworld.nic by Dhaval Patel, following Translation to make it correct grammatically.

  

If anybody can find the original printed Edition, that would be useful to confirm my corrections to the verse.
