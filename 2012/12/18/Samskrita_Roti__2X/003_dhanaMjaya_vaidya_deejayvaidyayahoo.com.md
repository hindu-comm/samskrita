चिन्तयामि - "रोटीरोटिका"शब्दौ हिन्द्या: संस्कृतं प्रति गतौ वा संस्कृताद्ध्हिन्द्याम् आगतौ ? मोनियरविल्यम्सकोशे "रोटिका"शब्दः भावप्रकाशनिघण्टुग्रन्थे प्राप्त इति उक्तम् । अयं निघण्टुग्रन्थः क्रिस्ताब्दे: 16तमे वर्षशतके रचितः । तस्मिन् निघण्टुग्रन्थे "फिरङ्ग"शब्दोऽपि दृश्यते ।  
  
Is it more likely that the Sanskrit word रोटी/रोटिका are back-imports from modern languages? The citation that Monier Williams gives for रोटिका is the 16th century भावप्रकाश (निघण्टु), which also includes the word "फिरङ्ग" meaning Frankish/European.  
  
No misunderstanding please - I have no objection that modern speakers of संस्कृत , and speakers during recent history have been importing words from (commonly spoken) modern languages. Just a matter of historical accuracy as to whether the word went from Hindi to near-historical संस्कृत, or went from ancient संस्कृत via प्राकृत steps into Hindi.
