Dear Anandji, Namaskar.  

A bit of a 'Teacherly' duty. Salam alekum is an illiterate's mispronunciation. It should be,"Assalaam alaikum",meaning " May God protect you". It is to be replied as,"Waalekum assalaam", meaning, " May God protect you too".  

Ram, Ram.
