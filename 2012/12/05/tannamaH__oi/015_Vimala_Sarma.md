The -ika ending can be made mas, fem or neuter, so neuter is ok.

If you use tasmai namaḥ, then the dative must be used for adj and shiva - sattvikāya śivāya.

In your anvaya, last bit यस्य सात्त्विकं शिवम् अस्ति is incorrect.

Here sāttvikam śivam is the accusative and object of numaḥ
