The breaking of the sandhi is right but tannamaH is not tasmai namaH in meaning.

It is "salutations to that sattvik shiva".

The sloka is numerating the four elements of abhinAya (from natyasAstra - angika, acika, Aharya, sattvika) as aplied to the supreme dancer, Shiva
