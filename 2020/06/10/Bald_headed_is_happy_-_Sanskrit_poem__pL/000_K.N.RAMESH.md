खल्वाटस्य महद् भाग्यं न श्रमो न धनव्यय: ।

अस्माकं केशिनां केशचिन्तायामयते वय: ।।

  

Lucky is the bald man, who needs no effort nor expenditure. For us, the hairy ones, our life is passing by in worrying about our hair.

  

Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
