So the correct way is to say:

  

अहम्, माम्, मया etc. have as their pronominal basis मद्;  
त्वम्, त्वाम्, र्वया, etc. have त्वद् as pronominal basis;  
वयम्, अस्मान्, अस्माभिः, etc. have अस्मद् as a pronominal basis;  
यूयम्, युष्मान्, युष्माभिः, etc. have pronominal basis युष्मद्;  

  

This is consistent with the compounds of the type:  

  

मदर्थम्, त्वदर्थम्, अस्मदर्थम्, युस्मदर्थम्  

  

where the first member of the compound is in thematic form. it's right?
