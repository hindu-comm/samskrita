विश्वनाथ

Those who know the difference between two knowers of the field ( क्षेत्रज्ञयोर् ), the jiva and paramatma, along with the field, and also the method of liberation (moksa) from prakrti for the living entities (bhuta), such as meditation, achieve the supreme goal.

  

बलदेव  

Those who know the difference ( अन्तरं) between the *jīva**,*** the Lord and the field of *prakåti,*(क्षेत्रेण सहितयोःक्षेत्रज्ञयोर्) by eyes which see the differences , using the method discussed by Me, and who know the process for liberation of the living entities from *prakṛti* , starting with *amānitva,* go to that place which is beyond *prakṛti,* My supreme attractive abode called the spiritual sky.

  

I get the idea of "two connoisseurs of the field" from the aforementioned comments. Are these translations not correct?
