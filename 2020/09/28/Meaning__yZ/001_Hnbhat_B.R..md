First try to write correct words:

  

गौरीवल्लभ कामारे कालकूटविषाशन । मामुद्धरापदम्भोधेस्त्रिपुरघ्नान्तकान्तक।।  

  

Split the words by sandhi rules if any :

  

गौरीवल्लभ कामारे कालकूटविषाशन । माम उद्धर आपदम्भोधेः त्रिपुरघ्न अन्तकान्तक।।  

  

Now translate word by word in order of syntax:

  

गौरीवल्लभ = O husband of Gauri, कामारे = Enemy of Kama, the god of love, कालकूटविषाशन = who devored the Kalakuta, the poison, त्रिपुरघ्न = destroyer of three cities (of the demons) अन्तकान्तक = the destroyer of Yamam (the god of death)!माम् उद्धर = Please uplift me आपदम्भोधेः = from the ocean of difficulties!  

  

Hope now the verse is clear.
