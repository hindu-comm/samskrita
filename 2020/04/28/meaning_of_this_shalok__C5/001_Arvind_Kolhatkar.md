The correct rendering of this verse as under:

  

वराप्सरस्सहस्राणि शूरमायोधने हतं । त्वरमाणाः प्रधावन्ति मम भर्ता भवेदिति ॥

  

A free translation of this verse will be:

  

Towards a brave man fallen in battle, thousands of celestial damsels run saying,'he shall be my husband.'

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
