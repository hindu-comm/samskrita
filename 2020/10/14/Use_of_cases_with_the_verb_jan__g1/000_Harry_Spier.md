In a sanskrit textbook reading exercise about King Sagara, there are these two sentences.

केशिन्याम् एकः पुत्रो ऽसमञ्ज इति ख्यातो ऽजायत\|

सुमत्यां गर्भतुम्बो ऽजायत \|

  

सुमत्यां andकेशिन्याम् are in the locative case. Is this correct sanskrit?  


Apte, in his dictionary, for theverb jan, says the source of birth is in the ablative case. Should these two sentences be:

केशिन्या एकः पुत्रो ऽसमञ्ज इति ख्यातो ऽजायत \|  

सुमत्या गर्भतुम्बो ऽजायत\|
