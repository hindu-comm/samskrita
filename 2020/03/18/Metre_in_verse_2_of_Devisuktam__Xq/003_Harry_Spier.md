Thank you Jagannatha,

  

Note: in the following G = guru, L = laghu, X = anything.  

MacDonell in his sanskrit grammar gives two admissable (though rare) forms of shloka metre where the 5th syllable is heavy.

a\) X G L G G L L X \| X X X X L G L X \|\|

b\) X G L G G G G X \| X X X X L G L X \|\|

  

But neither agrees with the meter of the first pada of the 2nd verse of the Devisuktam

  

रौद्रायै नमो नित्यायै गौर्यै धात्र्यै नमो नमः

which is G G G L G G G G \| G G G G L G L G \|\|
