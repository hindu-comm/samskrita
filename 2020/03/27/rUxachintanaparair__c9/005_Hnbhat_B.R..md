It is sandhi of two words मयि+अनाथे both in locative of अस्मद् and अनाथ. meaning on me helpless.

  

##### Shabda Sagara

अनाथ  
mfn. (-थः-था-थं) Without a master or protector, without a husband, &c.  
E. अ neg. नाथ master.

  

##### Capeller Eng

१ अनाथ a. having no protector, helpless.

२ अनाथ॑ n. helplessness.
