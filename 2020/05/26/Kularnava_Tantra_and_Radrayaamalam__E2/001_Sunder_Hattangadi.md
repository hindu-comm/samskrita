**About the Book:**

The Kularnava is perhaps the foremost[Tantra](https://www.exoticindiaart.com/article/tantric/)of the Kaula School and is constantly cited as an authority in Tantric literature. It is worthy of close study by those who would understand the tenets and practice of the tradition of which it is a Sastra.

The Introduction by Sir John Woodroffe (Arthur Avalon) gives a concise outline of the work.

Sri M.P. Pandit who is a keen student of the Tantras and[Vedas](https://www.exoticindiaart.com/article/understanding-vedas/)has rendered the work
