Dear All,  

  

Greetings and Namaste. As **Nrusimha Jayanti** and **Kurma Jayanti** fall on Wed (06-May-2020) and Thu (07-May-2020) respectively, I am glad to share a rare king of prayer on **Lord Vishnu** from **Nrusimha Puranam** and **Chapter 16**.

  

As the brief **phalashruti** mentions, one who recites this hymn in the morning begets emancipation and **Vaishnavi Siddhi**. **Lord Shankara** corroborates that the only way to cross the ocean of **Samsara** is to meditate on **Lord Vishnu** and get His grace.

  

May We Pray to Lord Narayana with this hymn.

  

With best regards and Pranams,
