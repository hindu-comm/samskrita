In Maurer's sanskrit grammar lesson 18 there is this sanskrit sentence in a description of the churning of the ocean story.

  

  

सर्वलोकपितामहो देवार्थं विष्णुमुपकारं अयाचत् \|"The grandfather of all the worlds asked Vishnu's help for the devas."

  

Isदेवार्थं in the correct case (accusative) ?

  

Or should it be in the genitive. I.e.

  

  

सर्वलोकपितामहो देवार्थास्योपकारं विष्णुमयाचत् \|

  

Is a compound also OK?

  

सर्वलोकपितामहो देवार्थोपकारं विष्णुमयाचत् \|
