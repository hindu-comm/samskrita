That was related to the part of your email " It is also unclear. Why स्वधा and स्वाहा are in चतुर्थी विभक्ति. The dictionary says: स्वाहा is indeclinable."

  

To understand why स्वधा नमः is used, we can take the help of the following mantra enshrined in 19/36 of Shukla Yajurveda Madhyandiniya Samhita. ( Please check the samhita of your shakha to be sure whether this mantra is there)  
  
पितृभ्यः स्वधायिभ्यःस्वधा नमः पितामहेभ्यः स्वधायिभ्यः स्वधा नमःप्रपितामहेभ्यः स्वधायिभ्य स्वधा नमः.... .... ...

  
Mahidhara gives this quotationस्वधा वै पितॄणामन्नम् in his bhashya,Soपितॄन् स्वधा नमः has the connotation I offer this अन्नम् to myपितॄन्
