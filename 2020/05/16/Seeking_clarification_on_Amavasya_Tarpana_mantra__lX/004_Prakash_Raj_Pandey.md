स्वाहा and स्वधाare two of the eleven deities known as एकादशगौर्यः (eleven Gauri-s ), and in our family, on the eighthday of the Navaratra, they each are separately invoked and worshiped with five offerings (पञ्चोपचारपूजा), and those who cannot do the elaborate puja cut it short and say सर्वोपचारार्थे गन्धाक्षतपुष्पाणि समर्पयामि ।

  
Here is the complete list of the eleven Gauri-s.   
  
जयन्ती मङ्गला काली भद्रकाली कपालिनी ।  
दुर्गा क्षमा शिवा धात्री स्वाहा स्वधा नमोऽस्तु ते ॥
