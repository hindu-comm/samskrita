Prof. Ganesh Ramakrishnan (Dept of CSE, IIT Bombay) and Prof. Ramasubramanian (Dept of Humanities and Social Sciences, IIT Bombay) are attempting to significantly speed up the process of digitization of Sanskrit texts.
