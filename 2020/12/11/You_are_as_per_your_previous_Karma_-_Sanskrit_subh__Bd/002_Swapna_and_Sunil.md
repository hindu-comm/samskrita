Hari OM@krismohi,

  

शयानं चानुशयति तिष्ठन्तं चानुतिष्ठति।

अनुधावति धावन्तं कर्म पूर्वकृतं नरम्॥

- **सुभाषितसुधानिधि**

  

A being's deeds of the past follow him in his sleep, follow him when standing, (as well as) follow him when running. (No respite!)
