It is not accusative of any noun.

  

The 'm' in the end is the common naada sound at the end of the beejaaksharasamong which 'hree' is one.

  

All beejaaksharas'aim', 'gam' , 'kleem' etc. all have that sound as the common end element.

  

You may learn more about mantras etc. from
