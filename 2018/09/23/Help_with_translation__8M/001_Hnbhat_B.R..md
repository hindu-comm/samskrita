This refutes असत्कार्यवाद of Naiyayikas. It is part of सत्कार्यवाद accepted by Vedantins.  
  
Split the sentence to make clear the sense.  
  
अयं भावः-जगतःसत्कारणत्वेअयं भावः-जगतोसत्कारणत्वे, घटः असन् , पटः सन् इत्यसत एव सर्वत्रानुवृत्तिस्स्यात्,न तु सतः\|

  

He says, "This is the intention. If the world is caused by non-being, the repetition of statement "the pot is not being there, the cloth is not being" will refer to to non-existent thing everywhere and not of the be to the extent thing.

  

This is rough translation.
