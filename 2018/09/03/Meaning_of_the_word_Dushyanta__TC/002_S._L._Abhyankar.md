I guess that the split-up of दुष्यन्त is दुष् + इ + अन्त.

Since दुष् means defect (Note, दोषः दुष्ट and दुष्टताare from दुष् only) and

अन्त means "ending"

दुष्यन्त should mean "one, who puts an end to all defects, (to all wickedness, hatred, enmity)
