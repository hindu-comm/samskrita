उभेउ हैवैष एते तरतिनैनं कृताकृते तपतः ॥ २२ ॥ is the text as printed in Samata Books edition (= Vani Vilas edṇ 1910). It also is same in Upanishadbrahman's bhashya.  

  

Kanva recension does give:

  

<https://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_upanishhat/bri.html?lang=sa>  

  

K ubhe u haivaiSha

एते॒ त॒रति अमृतः साध्वसाधु॒नी
