Durgaa sooktham is a hymn found in TaiththireeyaaraNyakam and also in MahaanaaraayaNA UpaniSHadh. It is an invocation to Durga Devi. It is generally recited when Abhisheka is performed to Goddess Parvati/Durga/consort of Lord Shiva or, during a Homa/Yagna. It can also be recited during Navaratri/Dusshera.
