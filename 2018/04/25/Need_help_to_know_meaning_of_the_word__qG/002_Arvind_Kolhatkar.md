Another variant known to me:

  

अनायासेन मरणं विना दैन्येन जीवितम् ।

अन्तकाले च गोविन्द स्मरणं देहि मे तव ॥

  

O Govind, bestow upon me these - death without suffering. life without misery and memory of you name at the time of expiry.

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
