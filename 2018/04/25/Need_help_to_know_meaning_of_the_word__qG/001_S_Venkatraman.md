अनायासेन मरणं

विना दैन्येन जीवनम्।

देहान्ते तव सान्निध्यम्

देहि मे परमेश्वर।।

  

Oh God, please grant me

Easy (effortless) death,

Life without having to depend on anyone,

and proximity to you when I depart from this body.

  

Regards,

Venkat
