कुलदैवतमस्माकं कोदण्डशरमण्डितम्।

इष्टदैअतमस्माकं इक्ष्वाकुकुलदैवतम्ं॥

  

The above verse is recited minimum 12 times before commencing Sundarakanda Parayanam of SRIMAD VALMKI RAMAYANAM.

This has been recommended by LATE BRAHMASRI ANANTHARAMA DIKSHITAR IN HIS BOOK.
