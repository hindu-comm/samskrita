There is a similar verse inभवभूति's उत्तररामचरित. Thinking of सीता as a child, her father जनक says:

  

अनियतरुदितस्मितं विराजत्कतिपयकोमलदन्तकुड्‍मलाग्रम् ।

वदनकमलकं शिशोः स्मरामि स्खलदसमञ्जसमुग्धजल्पितं ते ॥

उत्तररामचरित ४.४

  

(I remember the lotus-like face of the child which cries or smiles without any reason, which looks adorable because of the few young buds of teeth and which babbles lisping words bereft of meaning.)

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
