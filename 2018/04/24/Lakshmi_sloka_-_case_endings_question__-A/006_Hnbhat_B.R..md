# Mahalakshmi Ashtakam - In sanskrit with meaning

- from Padma Purana

  

The meaning:

  

Meaning:  
1.1:IWorshipfully SaluteDevi Mahalakshmi, Who is theMahamaya(the Primordial Cause of Creation); And Who isWorshippedinSri Pitha(Her Abode) by theSuras.  
1.2:(I Worshipfully Salute Her) Who is adorned in Her Beautiful Form withShankha(Conch),Chakra(Disc) andGada(Mace) in HerHands; IWorshipfully Salute Devi Mahalakshmi.
