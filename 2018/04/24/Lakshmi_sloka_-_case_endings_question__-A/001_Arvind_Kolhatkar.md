These are endings of the vocative caseसंबोधन विभक्ति for the female gender nouns ending in आ like महामाया, सुरपूजिता etc and correspond to ते in नमस्तेऽस्तु. Also,महालक्ष्मि is the vocative for महालक्ष्मी.

  

(I do not know how experts in Paninian grammar would put this across.
This is how I learnt it in school.)
