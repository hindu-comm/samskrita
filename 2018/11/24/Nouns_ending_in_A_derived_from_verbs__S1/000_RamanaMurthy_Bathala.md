Dear sir,

  

The following nouns are derived from verbs:

गोपा = cow-herd

विश्वपा = protector of the world

शंखध्मा = couch-shell blower

सोमपा= one who quaffs the soma-juice

धूम्रपा= one who inhales smoke

बलदा= strength-giver or Indra

  

I want to know whether they can be used as both masculine and feminine nouns:

  

That is:

  

विश्वपा (m. & f.)

शंखध्मा (m. & f.)

सोमपा (m. & f.)

धूम्रपा (m. & f.)

बलदा (m. & f.)

  

Regards

Ramana murthy
