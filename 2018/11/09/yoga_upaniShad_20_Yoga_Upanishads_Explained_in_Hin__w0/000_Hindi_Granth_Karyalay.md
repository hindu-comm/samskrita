The Upanishads are powerful statements of the Vedic dharma. They are connected with the Vedas and express the profound principles of Yoga in very simple Hindi.   
  
Here are the Yoga Upanishads, in Sanskrit text + Hindi translation and commentary:  
  
1. अमृतबिन्दूपनिषद् Amrita Bindu Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

2. अद्वयतारकोपनिषद् Advaya Taraka Upanishad {Shukla YAJURVEDA}

3. अमृतनादोपनिषद् Amrita Nada Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

4. क्षुरिकोपनिषद् Kshurika Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

5. तेजोबिन्दूपनिषद् Tejah Bindu Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

6. त्रिशिखिब्राह्मणोपनिषद् Trishikhi Brahmana Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

7. दर्शनोपनिषद् Darshana Upanishad {SAMAVEDA}

8. ध्यानबिन्दूपनिषद् Dhyana Bindu Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

9. नादबिन्दूपनिषद् Nada Bindu Upanishad {RIGVEDA}

10. पाशुपतब्रह्मोपनिषद् Pashupata Brahma Upanishad {ATHARVAVEDA}

11. ब्रह्मविद्योपनिषद् Brahma Vidya Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

12. मण्डलब्राह्मणोपनिषद् Mandala Brahmana Upanishad {Shukla YAJURVEDA}

13. महावाक्योपनिषद् Mahavakya Upanishad {ATHARVAVEDA}

14. योगकुण्डल्युपनिषद् Yoga Kundali Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

15. योगचूडामणि उपनिषद् Yoga Cudamani Upanishad {SAMAVEDA}

16. योगतत्त्वोपनिषद् Yoga Tattva Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

17. योगशिखोपनिषद् Yoga Shikha Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

18. वराहोपनिषद् Varaha Upanishad {Krishna YAJURVEDA}

19. शाण्डिल्योपनिषद् Shandilya Upanishad {ATHARVAVEDA}

20. हंसोपनिषद् Hamsa Upanishad {Shukla YAJURVEDA}

  

These Upanishads shed light upon the spiritual as well as esoteric principles and practices of Yoga. The reader would do well to study them. They are ideal for the religious devotee, the spiritual seeker, the yoga practitioner and the scholar of Asian Studies and World Religion.
