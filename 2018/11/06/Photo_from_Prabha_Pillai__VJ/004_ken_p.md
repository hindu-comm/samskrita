*yadachetano'pi pādai: spṛṣṭa:*

*prajvalati saviturinakānta: \|*

*tattejasvī puruṣa: parakṛtavikṛtï kathȧ sahate \|\|*

*  
*

*When even an inanimate object like jasper becomes hot with the touch of sun beams, then how can a brilliant man brook any affront from others ?*

*  
*

*sāhityasȧ gītakalāvihīnaḥ  
*

*sākṣātpashuḥ puchchhaviṣāṇahīnaḥ \|*

*tṛṇȧ na khādannapi jīvamānaḥ*

*tadbhāgadheyȧ paramȧ pashūnām \|\|*

*  
*

*Jis vyakti ke pās sāhity nahī̇ , sȧgīt nahī̇ aur kalā bhī nahī̇ , vaha pū̇chh aur shī̇g bagair kā pashu hī hai \| Ghās n khākar vah jīvit rahatā hai, yah sachamuch pashuȯ kā param bhāgy hai \|*
