[[K.N.RAMESH	2018-11-06, 00:12:49 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/OgoFJRl42l4)]]



Courtesy: Sri.K.Muralidharan Iyengar  
  

Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste.

  

**May I seize this opportunity to wish you, your family, kith and kin, friends and all a very Happy and Prosperous Deepavali. May The Lord, Who is Jytoirupa, illuminate our hearts and intellect with true Gyana and wisdom and bestow all we rightfully aspire for!**

  

I have pleasure in attaching a short and sweet prayer on **Lord Krishna by Akrura** taken from **Brahma Vaivarta Puranam, Krishna Janma Khanda, and Chapter 70**. The brief **Phalashruti** claims that one recites this every day will beget progeny, wealth, land, comfort, etc.

  

Once enjoying the best of best festivities during this Deepavali season!
