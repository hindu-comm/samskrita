Not that I care whether kapi has a Sanskrit origin or not, but this declaration does not seem to flow logically.  

What I know is this:  

\- According to [dictionary.com](http://dictionary.com), the word origns of both monkey and ape are obscure with no reference to a Sanskrit counterpart.  

\- The word kapi itself is quite old in Sanskrit usage and can be seen in the Rig Veda. E.g., Vrshaakapi (sun? agni? something else?)  

\- I have no idea of how the modern Armenian word kapik evolved. Without knowing more, I don't think we can say anything definitely about its origin in relation to kapi. For example, just because the word "loot" is in English, would we attribute an English origin?
