Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. As **Sri Varaha Jayanti** falls on T**hursday, 05-Apr-2018**, I am delighted to share a very rare and beautiful hymn on **Lord Yajna Varaha by Goddess Earth** (and as told by **Sage Shaunaka**) taken from **Matsya Puranam** and **Chapter 248** titled
**Varaha Praturbhava**). In this hymn **Goddess Earth** explains the important names of Lord Narayana and corroborates that forms like 12 Adityas, 11 Rudras, etc. are actually **Lord Narayana** in different forms.  

  

In the brief **Phalashruti** at the end, **Lord Varaha** declares that one who recites this hymn will never see defeat or setback in all his rightful endeavors and becomes dear to the sages and a blemishless Vaishnavite.

  

May We pray to **Lord Yajnavaraha** on **Sri Varaha Jayanti**.
