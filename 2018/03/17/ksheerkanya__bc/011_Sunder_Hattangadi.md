Shrikhand is an Indian sweet dish made of strained yogurt.

  

History

Shrikhand has been referred to as "Shikhrini" in the

Sanskrit literature. According to Jashbhai B. Prajappati

and Baboo M. Nair, it originated in ancient India,

around 400 B.C.E..\[1\]
