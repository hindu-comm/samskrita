We're well into hair-splitting at this point.  

  

\> in Samskritam there are three types of words - तिङ्ङन्त, सुबन्त, अव्यय.

  

Leaving aside how the sutras are framed, this is a perfectly fine big-picture understanding. For example, the introductory "vyakarana praveshika" says: संस्कृतभाषायां पदानि त्रिधा विभक्तानि। शब्दः क्रिया अव्ययम् इति॥
