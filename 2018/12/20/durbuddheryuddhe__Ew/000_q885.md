Namō vaḥ sarvēbhyaḥ,

  

This may be a stupid question, but I will ask it anyway.

  

I was just reading Bhagavad-Gītā when I came upon this line:

योत्स्यमानानवेक्षेऽहं य एतेऽत्र समागताः ।

धार्तराष्ट्रस्य दुर्बुद्धेर्युद्धे प्रियचिकीर्षवः ॥१-२३॥ (per[https://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/भगवद्गीता/अर्जुनविषादयोगः](https://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/भगवद्गीता/अर्जुनविषादयोगः))

  

I would assume durbuddhēryuddhē divides asdur-buddhēr yuddhē. I am not familiar with a declension dur-buddhēḥ. Would it beṣaṣṭhī and go with Dhārtarāṣṭrasya?

  

Please forgive my ignorance, but is this an alternate form?

  

Dhanyavādaḥ
