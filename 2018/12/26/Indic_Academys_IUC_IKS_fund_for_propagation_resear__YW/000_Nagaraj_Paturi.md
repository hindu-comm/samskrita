[[Nagaraj Paturi	2018-12-26, 15:56:55 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/YWbQmePtT1Y)]]



Indic Academy is constituting a long term fund with an initial corpus of Rs. Twenty Lakhs for propagation, research and publication of studies in different areas of Indic history, culture and religion . The Editorial Board will soon announce the various themes of research and scholarships open to students and researchers.

  

This fund will be administered under the aegis of the Inter University Center for Indic Knowledge Systems of Indic Academy.  

  

The fund will be managed by an Independent Editorial board consisting of Shri. Koenraad Elst as the Chairman and Shri. Shrikant Taalegri, Shri. R. Jagannathan and Smt. Meenakshi Jain as the other members.  

  

The fund is named asthe Ram Swarup- Sitaram Goel Memorial Fund for research and publication.
