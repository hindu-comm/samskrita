Verily,   
  
पृथिव्यां त्रिणि रत्नानि जलमन्नं सुभाषितम्

मूढैः पाशाणखण्डेषु रत्नसंज्ञाऽभिधीयते

  

prithivyaam trini ratnaani jalamannam subhaashitam  
mudhaih paashaanakhandeshu ratnasamjnaabhidhiiyate  
  
There are only three gems in this world

Water, food and words of wisdom  
Those who call bits of rock as gems  
Are fools  
  

Sanskrit is one of the greatest contributions of India to mankind. It encompasses some of the best writings in the world in fields as diverse as poetry, philosophy, dramatics, spirituality, yoga, ayurveda, self development, politics, economics and erotica.   
  
For thousands of years, Sanskrit has expressed the religious, social, political, economic and cultural worldviews and aspirations of our motherland.

  

The name Sanskrit means refined, consecrated and sanctified. Sanskrit is the classical language of India and the liturgical language of Hinduism, Jainism and Buddhism. It is one of the 22 official languages of India.  
  
The Subhashita-Ratna-Bhandagaram is a compilation of more than 10,000 subhashitas (wise sayings) from Sanskrit literature compiled and arranged subjectwise by Shri Kashinath Sharma. It was published by the fabled Nirnayasagara Press in 1952. In this edition, the Sanskrit subhashita is followed by an excellent Hindi translation; which makes it accessible to a large audience.   
  
This book is ideal for all those who love language and relish its elegant usage. It is a must have for public orators. We are proud to make this book available at our bookstore and through mail order. We ship books all over the world and accept payment by IMPS and NEFT, Visa and MasterCard.
