[[Arvind_Kolhatkar	2018-12-06, 10:05:51 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/Qsz3vwWsPTU)]]



Though the compiler of Subhashita ratna bhandagar is shown at some places as Kashinath Sharma, he was actually Kashinath Panduranga Parab, a pundit who was often associated with another pandit by name Vasudev Laxman Panashikar in Sanskrit books brought out by Javji Dadaji of the Nirnayasagar Press.

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
