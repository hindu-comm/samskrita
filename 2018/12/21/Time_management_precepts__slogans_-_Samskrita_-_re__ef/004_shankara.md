Namaste,

  

3\. Swami Vivekananda's quote "Arise Awake ....", has been translated into Sanskrit prose as well as poetry as found in Sanskrit works on Swami Vivekananda.

  

a\. Prose translation from 'Uttishthata Jagrita' (Sanskrit translation of Eknath Ranade's compilation of SV teachings titled A Rousing Call to Hindu Nation), page 107.

  

उत्तिष्ठन्तु। जागरिताः भवन्तु। लक्ष्यप्राप्तिपर्यन्तं निरन्तरं परिश्राम्यन्तु।

<https://archive.org/details/UttishthataJagritaTranslationOfRousingCallToHinduNationNagaratnaHegade2004RSSHR/page/n123>

  

b\. Translation into Sanskrit verse in Swami Apurvananda's 'Yugacharya Vivekananda' page 332.

  

उत्तिष्ठ जाग्रहि द्राक्त्वं स्वप्नेनालं तवाधुना।  
स्वलक्ष्यप्राप्तिपर्यन्तमुद्यमं न परित्यजेः॥  
  
<https://archive.org/details/YugacharyaVivekanandaSwamiApurvananda/page/n341>
