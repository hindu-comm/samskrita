[[Saffron Heart ॥ केसरहृदयम् ॥	2018-12-21, 22:23:01 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/TaCEUZYfFxw)]]



A short speech from Attack on Titan Episode 10 :- <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y4KXMxVdBfY> (Attack on Titan Episode 10 Spoilers)  
  
**Speech :-**  
  

> योद्धाहम् !  
> yoddhāham !  
> I am a soldier!  
>   
> मानुष्यकृते मम हृदयं समर्पितं मम भोः !  
> mānuṣyakr̥te mama hr̥dayaṁ samarpitaṁ mama bhoḥ !  
> I have dedicated my heart for humanity, sir!  
>   
> न तु अन्यार्थाय मरणाय बहुगर्वितो़ऽहं, यावत् एतदार्थाय।  
> na tu anyārthāya maraṇāya bahugarvito़'haṁ, yāvat etadārthāya।  
> Dying for no other cause can make me prouder than this cause!  
>   
> यदि तस्य महाकायशक्तेः प्रयोजनं कृत्वा,  
> yadi tasya mahākāyaśakteḥ prayojanaṁ kr̥tvā,  
> If we used his titan power,  
>   
> अस्माकं शेषसेनाबलेन सह संयोजनं कृतम् ,  
> asmākaṁ śeṣasenābalena saha saṁyojanaṁ kr̥tam ,  
> and combined it with our remaining manpower,  
>   
> तर्हि एतस्य नगरस्य प्रतिग्रह्णं शक्यम् !  
> tarhi etasya nagarasya pratigrahṇaṁ śakyam !  
> then it's possible to retake this city!  
>   
> इति मन्ये अहम् !  
> iti manye aham !  
> I believe so!  
>   
> मानुष्यवैभवाय ,  
> mānuṣyavaibhavāya ,  
> For the glory of humanity,  
>   
> समर्थनायश्च तस्य ,  
> samarthanāyaśca tasya ,  
> and in his support,  
>   
> मम मृत्युपूर्वे -- एतत् अहम् भाषे !!!  
> mama mr̥tyupūrve -- etat aham bhāṣe !!!  
> before I die -- I speak thus!!!

  
-----------------------------------------  
  
**Translating the series title :-  
  
**The original title in japanese "Shingeki no Kyojin" = Advancing/Attacking Titan.  
  
Shingeki = Advancing/Attacking ---> अभ्यमित्रीय = अभि + अमित्रीय (meaning advancing/attacking against enemies)  
Kyojin = lit. big-man/giant = Titan ---> महाकाय (giant)  
  
Thus series title can be "अभ्यमित्रीयमहाकाय".
