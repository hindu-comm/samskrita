*Oṃasya shrī prāṇa pratiṣṭhā mȧtrasya bramhā viṣṇu maheshvarā: ṛṣayaḥ\| ṛgyaju: sāmāni chhȧdā̇si\| kriyāmaya vapu: prāṇa shakti devatā\| aï
bījam‌\| hrī̐ shakti:\| krī̐ kīlam‌\| prāṇa pratiṣṭhāpane viniyogaḥ\|  
*

*  
*

*Ṛṣyādi Nyāsa-  
*

*  
*

*Oṃbramhā viṣṇu maheshvarā: ṛṣibhyo namaḥ shirasi\|  
*

*  
*

*ṛgyajuḥ sāmachchhandebhyo namaḥ mukhe\|*

*  
*

*prāṇashaktyai namaḥ hradaye\|*

*  
*

*ai̐ bījāya namaḥ li̐ge\|*

*  
*

*hrī̐ shaktaye namaḥ pādayoḥ\|*

*  
*

*krī̐ kīlakāya namaḥ sarvā̐geṣu\|*

*  
*

*Kara Nyāsa-  
*

*  
*

*Oṃȧ kȧ khȧ gȧ ghȧ ṅȧ ā̇ pṛthivyaptejo vāyvākāshātmane ȧ guṣṭhābhyā̇ namaḥ\|  
*

*  
*

*Oṃï chȧ chhȧ jȧ jhȧ ñȧ ī̐ shabdasparsha rupa-rasa-gȧ dhātmane tarjanībhyā̇ namaḥ\|*

*  
*

*Oṃu̇ ṭȧ ṭhȧ ḍȧ ḍhȧ ṇȧ ū̇ tvak‌ chakṣuḥ shrotra jihvāghrāṇātmane madhyamābhyā̇ namaḥ\|*

*  
*

*Oṃė tȧ thȧ dȧ dhȧ nȧ ai̐ vāk‌ pāṇi pāda pāyūpasthātmane anāmikābhyā̇ namaḥ\|*

*  
*

*Oṃo̐ pȧ phȧ bȧ bhȧ mȧ au̐ vachanādāna gati visargā nȧ dātmane kaniṣṭhikābhyā̇ namaḥ\|*

*  
*

*Oṃȧ yȧ rȧ lȧ vȧ shȧ ṣȧ sȧ hȧ kṣȧ aḥ mano buddhayahȧ kāra chitta vijñānātmane karatalakara pṛṣṭhābhyā̇ namaḥ\|*

*  
*

*Hṛdayādi Nyāsa-*

*  
*

*Oṃȧ kȧ khȧ gȧ ghȧ ṅȧ ā̇ pṛthivyaptejo vāyvākāshātmane hṛdayāya namaḥ\|  
*

*  
*

*Oṃï chȧ chhȧ jȧ jhȧ ñȧ ī̐ shabdasparsha rupa-rasa-gȧ dhātmane shirase svāhā\|*

*  
*

*Oṃu̇ ṭȧ ṭhȧ ḍȧ ḍhȧ ṇȧ ū̇ tvak‌ chakṣuḥ shrotra jihvāghrāṇātmane shikhāyai vaṣaṭ‌\|*

*  
*

*Oṃė tȧ thȧ dȧ dhȧ nȧ ai̐ vāk‌ pāṇi pāda pāyūpasthātmane kavachāya hu̇\|*

*  
*

*Oṃo̐ pȧ phȧ bȧ bhȧ mȧ au̐ vachanādāna gati visargā nȧ dātmane netratrayāya vauṣaṭ‌\|*

*  
*

*Oṃȧ yȧ rȧ lȧ vȧ shȧ ṣȧ sȧ hȧ kṣȧ aḥ mano buddhayahȧ kāra chitta vijñānātmane astrāya phaṭ‌\|*

*  
*

*Oṃai̐ iti nābhimārabhya pādāntȧ spṛshet‌\|*

*  
*

*Oṃhrī̐ iti hṛdayamārabhya nābhyantam‌ spṛsheta\|*

*  
*

*Oṃkrī̐ iti mastakamārabhya hṛdayā̇ tȧ cha spṛsheta\|*

*  
*

*Dhyāna-  
*

*  
*

*raktāmbhodhistha potollasadaruṇa sarojāviruddhā karābjaiḥ,  
*

*  
*

*pāshȧ kodaṇḍa bhikṣud‌bhavamatha guṇa mapyȧ atya kushȧ pa̐cha bāṇān‌\|*

*  
*

*bibhrāṇā̇ sṛkkapālȧ trinayana lasitā pīna vakṣoruhāḍh‌yāḥ,*

*  
*

*devī bālārka varṇā bhavatu sukhakarī prāṇashaktiḥ parā naḥ\|\|*

*  
*

*rāṇashaktiḥ parā naḥ\|\|*

*  
*

*Prāṇa Pratiṣṭhā Mȧtra-  
*

*  
*

*Oṃā̇ hrī̐ krau̐ yȧ rȧ lȧ vȧ shȧ ṣȧ sȧ hȧ saḥ so'ham‌ prāṇā iha prāṇāḥ\|  
*

*  
*

*Oṃā̇ hrī̐ krau̐ yȧ rȧ lȧ vȧ shȧ ṣȧ sȧ hȧ saḥ jīva iha jīva sthitaḥ\|*

*  
*

*Oṃā̇ hrī̐ krau̐ yȧ rȧ lȧ vȧ shȧ ṣȧ sȧ hȧ saḥ sarvė driyāṇi iha sarvė driyāṇi\|*

*  
*

*vāṅ‌manastvak‌ chakṣuḥ shrotra jihvā ghrāṇa vākprāṇa pād‌pāyūpasthāni ihaivāgatya sukhȧ chirȧ tiṣṭhȧ tu svāhā\|*

*  
*

*Oṃ(15 times) mama dehasya pȧ chadasha sȧ skārāḥ sampadyantām‌ ityuktvā\|*
