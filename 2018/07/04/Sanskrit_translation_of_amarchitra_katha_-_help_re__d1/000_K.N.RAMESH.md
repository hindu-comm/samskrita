Amar Chitra Katha in Sanskrit

  

Dear Friends,

After a long trial of about two years, it has been possible that the Amar Chitra Katha now can be available in Sanskrit. Vanda Mataram Library Trust (VMLT) would like to invite translators to translate first the most popular titles into Sanskrit. Once the review committee is satisfied with the translation, the books will be published and made available to all Sanskrit lovers and learners.
