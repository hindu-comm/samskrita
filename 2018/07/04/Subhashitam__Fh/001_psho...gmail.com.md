अनवसरे च यदुक्तं सुभाषितं तच्च भवति हास्याय

रहसि प्रौढवधूनां रतिसमये वेदपाठ इव

anavasare ca yaduktaṃ subhāṣitaṃ tacca bhavati hāsyāya  

rahasi prauḍhavadhūnāṃ ratisamaye vedapāṭha iva  

  

Just like a subhashitam spoken at an inopportune moment leads to ridicule, so does a veda lesson imparted during lovemaking to an impetuous bride
