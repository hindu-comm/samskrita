But as already suggested, if you are sending it to a research journal, the journal has a policy , you are expected to follow that.

  

A book that contains guidelines for such issues of presenting research publications is called a style manual or style sheet.

  

Some journals mention a particular style manual and ask you to follow that.

  

<https://archive.org/details/mlastylemanualgu00giba>  

  

<http://www.chicagomanualofstyle.org/tools_citationguide.html>  

  

Suitable modifications in these for Indian languages have been attempted.
