तरणी is boat. तरणि is sun.

  

तरणीis obviously from तर= to cross.

  

तरणि = sun itself is mentioned to have the meaning ray.

  

If they are from तर= to cross., it could be east to west crossing (for sun) or source to end travelling (for ray).

  

or तरणि = sun has some other etymology.
