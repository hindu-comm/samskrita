# मा भैष्ट 2Ap-लुँङ्

By[avg-sanskrit-class](https://avg-sanskrit.org/author/avg-sanskrit-class/ "View all posts by avg-sanskrit-class")in[Example for the day](https://avg-sanskrit.org/category/example-series/),[SB](https://avg-sanskrit.org/category/%e0%a4%b6%e0%a5%8d%e0%a4%b0%e0%a5%80%e0%a4%ae%e0%a4%a6%e0%a5%8d%e0%a4%ad%e0%a4%be%e0%a4%97%e0%a4%b5%e0%a4%a4%e0%a4%ae%e0%a5%8d/)on[April 19, 2012](https://avg-sanskrit.org/2012/04/19/%e0%a4%ae%e0%a4%be-%e0%a4%ad%e0%a5%88%e0%a4%b7%e0%a5%8d%e0%a4%9f-2ap-%e0%a4%b2%e0%a5%81%e0%a4%81%e0%a4%99%e0%a5%8d/ "10:10 pm").

Today we will look at the form**मा भैष्ट**2Ap-लुँङ् from श्रीमद्भागवतम् 10.34.28.

मा भैष्टेत्यभयारावौ शालहस्तौ तरस्विनौ । आसेदतुस्तं तरसा त्वरितं गुह्यकाधमम् ।। १०-३४-२८ ।।  
स वीक्ष्य तावनुप्राप्तौ कालमृत्यू इवोद्विजन् । विसृज्य स्त्रीजनं मूढः प्राद्रवज्जीवितेच्छया ।। १०-३४-२९ ।।

**श्रीधर-स्वामि-टीका**–  
**मा भैष्ट**भयं न प्राप्नु**तेत्य**भयवाचौ ।।२८।। ।।२९।।

*Gita Press translation – Shouting Their assurance of safety in the words “Do not be afraid!” with a sal tree in hand, the two nimble-footed Brothers ran with quick steps and speedily overtook that vile Guhyaka (Yakṣa). (28) Shuddering to perceive the two Brothers, close upon his heels like Kāla (the Time-Spirit) and Death personified, the fool abandoned the womenfolk and ran post-haste with intent to save his life.(29)*

‘मा भैष्ट’ is derived from the धातुः**[√भी](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/dhats/%E0%A4%AD%E0%A5%80)(ञिभी भये, जुहोत्यादि-गणः, धातु-पाठः # ३. २)**

The “ञि” at the beginning of “ञिभी” gets इत्-सञ्ज्ञा by[1-3-5](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-5.html)आदिर्ञिटुडवःand takes लोप: by[1-3-9](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-9.html)तस्य लोपः।

[√भी](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/dhats/%E0%A4%AD%E0%A5%80)is devoid of any indications for bringing in आत्मनेपद-प्रत्यया:। (Neither[1-3-12](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-12.html)अनुदात्तङित आत्मनेपदम्nor[1-3-72](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-72.html)स्वरितञितः कर्त्रभिप्राये क्रियाफलेapplies.) Therefore, as per[1-3-78](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-78.html)शेषात् कर्तरि परस्मैपदम्, in कर्तरि प्रयोग:[√भी](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/dhats/%E0%A4%AD%E0%A5%80)takes the परस्मैपद-प्रत्यया: by default. As per[1-4-99](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-4-99.html)लः परस्मैपदम्,[1-4-100](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-4-100.html)तङानावात्मनेपदम्, the nine प्रत्यया: from “तिप्” to “मस्” get the परस्मैपद-सञ्ज्ञा। So[√भी](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/dhats/%E0%A4%AD%E0%A5%80)can take only one of these nine प्रत्यया: in कर्तरि प्रयोग:।

The विवक्षा is**लुँङ्, कर्तरि प्रयोग:, मध्यम-पुरुषः, बहुवचनम्**।

\(1\) माङ् भी + लुँङ् । By[3-3-175](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/3-3-175.html)माङि लुङ्, the affix लुँङ् must be used after a verbal root when used in connection with the अव्ययम् “माङ्”।  
Note: This rule debars the use of any other लकार: after a verbal root when used in connection with the अव्ययम् “माङ्”। See question 2.

\(2\) मा भी + ल् । अनुबन्ध-लोपः by[1-3-2](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-2.html)उपदेशेऽजनुनासिक इत्,[1-3-3](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-3.html)हलन्त्यम्,[1-3-9](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-9.html)तस्य लोपः।

\(3\) मा भी + थ ।[3-4-78](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/3-4-78.html)तिप्तस्झिसिप्थस्थमिब्वस्मस्तातांझथासाथांध्वमिड्वहिमहिङ्mandates the प्रत्ययः “थ” as the substitute for the लकारः।

\(4\) मा भी + त । By[3-4-101](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/3-4-101.html)तस्थस्थमिपां तांतंतामः, the तिङ्-प्रत्ययाः “तस्”, “थस्”, “थ” and “मिप्” of a लकारः which is a ङित्, are replaced by “ताम्”, “तम्”, “त” and “अम्” respectively.

\(5\) मा भी + च्लि + त । By[3-1-43](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/3-1-43.html)च्लि लुङि, when लुँङ् follows, the प्रत्यय: “च्लि” is prescribed after a verbal root.  
Note: This सूत्रम् is a अपवाद: for[3-1-68](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/3-1-68.html)कर्तरि शप्‌etc.

\(6\) मा भी + सिँच् + त । By[3-1-44](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/3-1-44.html)च्लेः सिच्, the affix “च्लि” is substituted by “सिँच्”।

\(7\) मा भी + स् + त । अनुबन्ध-लोपः by[1-3-2](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-2.html)उपदेशेऽजनुनासिक इत्,[1-3-3](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-3.html)हलन्त्यम्,[1-3-9](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/1-3-9.html)तस्य लोपः।

Note:[7-2-10](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/7-2-10.html)एकाच उपदेशेऽनुदात्तात्‌stops the “इट्”-आगम: (for the प्रत्यय: “स्”) which would have been done by[7-2-35](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/7-2-35.html)आर्धधातुकस्येड् वलादेः।

\(8\) मा भै + स् + त । By[7-2-1](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/7-2-1.html)सिचि वृद्धिः परस्मैपदेषु– (The ending letter of) a base (अङ्गम्) which ends in a इक् letter takes the वृद्धि: substitute when followed by the affix सिँच् which is in turn followed by a परस्मैपदम् affix.

Note: By[6-4-74](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/6-4-74.html)न माङ्योगे, when used in connection with माङ्, a base (अङ्गम्) shall not take the augment अट् or आट्।[6-4-74](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/6-4-74.html)prohibits[6-4-71](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/6-4-71.html)लुङ्लङ्लृङ्क्ष्वडुदात्तः।

\(9\) मा भैष्त । By[8-3-59](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/8-3-59.html)आदेशप्रत्यययो:, the letter “स्” is replaced by the cerebral “ष्” when preceded either by a letter of the इण्-प्रत्याहार: or a letter of the क-वर्ग: (“क्”, “ख्”, “ग्”, “घ्”, “ङ्”)। This substitution only takes place if the “स्” is an आदेश: (substitute) or part of a प्रत्यय: (affix.)

\(10\) मा भैष्ट । By[8-4-41](http://avg-sanskrit.org/sutras/8-4-41.html)ष्टुना ष्टुः, when the letter “स्” or a letter of the त-वर्ग: (“त्”, “थ्”, “द्”, “ध्”, “न्”) comes in contact with either the letter “ष्” or a letter of the ट-वर्ग: (“ट्”, “ठ्”, “ड्”, “ढ्”, “ण्”) then it is replaced respectively by “ष्”, ट-वर्ग: (“ट्”, “ठ्”, “ड्”, “ढ्”, “ण्”).
