[[Nagaraj Paturi	2018-07-02, 09:17:08 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/wjCQVxCQ1p0)]]



Where ourपापis glossed there in 37, as**सुखिन:**?  

  

nihatya dhārtarāṣhṭrān naḥ kā prītiḥ syāj janārdana  
***pāpam*** evāśhrayed asmān hatvaitān ātatāyinaḥ  
tasmān nārhā vayaṁ hantuṁ dhārtarāṣhṭrān sa-bāndhavān  
sva-janaṁ hi kathaṁ hatvā ***sukhinaḥ*** syāma mādhava

[nihatya](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/nihatya)—by killing;[dhārtarāṣhṭrān](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/dhartarashtran)—the sons of Dhritarashtra;[naḥ](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/nah)—our;[kā](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/ka)—what;[prītiḥ](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/pritih)—pleasure;[syāt](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/syat)—will there be;[janārdana](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/janardana)—he who looks after the public, Shree Krishna;***[pāpam](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/papam)—vices***;[eva](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/eva)—certainly;[āśhrayet](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/ashrayet)—must come upon;[asmān](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/asman)—us;[hatvā](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/hatva)—by killing;[etān](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/etan)—all these;[ātatāyinaḥ](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/atatayinah)—aggressors;[tasmāt](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/tasmat)—hence;[na](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/na)—never;[arhāḥ](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/arhah)—behoove;[vayam](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/vayam)—we;[hantum](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/hantum)—to kill;[dhārtarāṣhṭrān](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/dhartarashtran)—the sons of Dhritarashtra;[sva-bāndhavān](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/sva-bandhavan)—along with friends;[sva-janam](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/sva-janam)—kinsmen;[hi](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/hi)—certainly;[katham](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/katham)—how;[hatvā](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/hatva)—by killing;***[sukhinaḥ](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/sukhinah)—happy***;[syāma](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/syama)—will we become;[mādhava](https://www.holy-bhagavad-gita.org/w/madhava)—Shree Krishna, the husband of Yogmaya
