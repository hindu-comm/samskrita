काकयाचकयोर्मध्ये वरः काको न याचकः

काक आह्वायते काकान् याचको न तु याचकान्  

  

kākayācakayormadhye varaḥ kāko na yācakaḥ  

kāka āhvāyate kākān yācako na tu yācakān  

  

Meaning: Between a crow and a beggar, a crow is better. A crow calls(invites) other crows whereas a beggar doesn't.
