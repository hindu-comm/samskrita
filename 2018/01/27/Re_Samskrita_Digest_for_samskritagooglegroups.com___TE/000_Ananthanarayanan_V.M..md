[[Ananthanarayanan V.M.	2018-01-27, 06:52:53 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/TEd30nc-wFc)]]



Raghuvamsam, Kumarasbhavam and meghadansesam have been translated in full by Prof V S VENKATARAGHAVACHARYA and are available for sale from the Lifco publishers, Ranganathan Street T Nagar Chennai 600017. Abhijnana sakuntalam has been translated by Maraimalai Adigal, from Saivasiddhanta publications, Tirunelveli, long time back. Copies can be borrowed from well known libraries of Madras (Chennai). Alliance Publications, Mylapore (Tank), Chennai, has published many a translations of Sanskrit works. A search in the library of RKM Vivekananda College, Mylapore, Chennai, would help a lot.
