Leaving aside life, one should guard self-respect. Breath is uncertain and transient. But honour is lasting till moon and stars.

\[This subhAshita stresses on importance of honour. That does not mean that life is unimportant altogether. Whenever malice touches their honour, they tend to disregard even their lives.\]
