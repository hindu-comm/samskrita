करोतौ is notद्विवचन of करोत् which has no meaning, but it isसप्तमी एकवचन of करोति,which means the root कृ as per theवार्त्तिकम् –इक्श्तिपौधातुनिर्देशे इति वक्तव्यम्। under 3-3-108 which means when the rootकृ is used. The total meaning will be: When the rootकृ is used after the upsarga-s सम्, परि/उप, meaning to adornभूषणit will get सुट् as explained by Abhyankar.
