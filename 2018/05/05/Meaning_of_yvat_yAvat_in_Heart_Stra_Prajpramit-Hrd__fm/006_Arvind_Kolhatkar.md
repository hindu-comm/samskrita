In the बीजगणित of भास्कराचार्य, under the topic called वर्णषड्विध, is the following verse:

  

यावत्तावत्कालको नीलकोऽन्यो

वर्ण: पीतो लोहितश्चैतदाद्या:।

अव्यक्तानां कल्पिता मानसंज्ञा-

स्तत्सङ्ख्यानं कर्तुमाचार्यवर्यै: ॥२१\|\|

Respected teachers have assigned terms such as यावत्तावत्, कालक, नीलक and other colours like पीत, लोहित etc when they deal with calculations of the unknowns.

  

Several exercises appear below this. One of them, No 28, is as under:

  

यावत्तावत्पञ्चकं व्येकरूपं यावत्तावद्भिस्त्रिभि: सद्विरूपै: ।

संगुण्य द्राग्ब्रूहि गुण्यं गुणं वा व्यस्तं स्वर्णं कल्पयित्वा च विद्वन् ॥

  

(यावत्तावत् stands for an unknown quantity, like 'x' of modern mathematics. रूप stands for the known number '1'. With this explanation, the exercise is as under:)

  

O learned person, tell me the product of २या - १ and ३या + २. Also work out the product by changing signs (+ to - and - to +) in the multiplicand or the multiplier.

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
