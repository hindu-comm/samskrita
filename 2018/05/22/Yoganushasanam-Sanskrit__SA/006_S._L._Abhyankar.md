Even if you are doing cut-and-paste, there are typos. I hope, they are only typos and not mistakes. Whatever the case, certainly they cannot be liked.

1.  \*केषाञ्चचन पदानामर्थः\*  

1.  There is oneच extra. 2.  \*केषाञ्चन पदानाम् \* are plural; but अर्थः is singular😟  

"... योगशास्त्रस्य याथर्थ्येन निरूपणं अत्र क्रीयते इति । ..."

1.  क्रीयते should be क्रियते !
2.  क्रीयते would mean "is being bought, purchased😟That is
    ridiculous😟in the given context.
3.  याथर्थ्येन should be या**था**र्थ्येन  

Your doing cut-and-paste cannot be an excuse for your not being alert and attentive to the mistakes. It becomes your responsibility to correct the mistakes before posting.
