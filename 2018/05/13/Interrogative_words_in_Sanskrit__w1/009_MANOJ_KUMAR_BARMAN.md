All interrogative sense may be divided into two part : one is affirmative or negative answer giving Interrogation and another is explanatory answer givingInterrogation. For instance:

  

Q: अपि कुशली भवान्? (Are you OK?)

Ans: आम् /न (Yes/No)

Q: किं राम: विद्यालयं गच्छति? (Has Ram gone to school?)

Ans:आम् /न (Yes/No)

  

Q: सीता कुत्र गच्छति? (Where is Sita going?)

Ans: सीता हट्टं गच्छति। (Sita is going to market.)
