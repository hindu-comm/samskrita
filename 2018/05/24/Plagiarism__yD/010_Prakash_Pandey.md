What a ghostwriter writes is published in other person's name, the ghostwriter is absent from the text. But a plagiarist will pick from other's text without giving due credit to the original writer. So a ghostwriter is प्रच्छन्नरचयिता, and a plagiarist a चौररचयिता (?).
