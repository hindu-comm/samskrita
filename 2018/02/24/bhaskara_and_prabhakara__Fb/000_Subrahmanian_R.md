Respected scholars,

  

The words भास्कर and प्रभाकर are from the same root भा to shine, and mean the same - 'the Sun'.

I think in this context the उपसर्ग प्र has not changed the meaning significantly or drastically.

How come in one instance the augment स is there but not in the other?
