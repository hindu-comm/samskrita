काकयाचकयोर्मध्ये

वरं काको न याचक: /

काक आह्वयते काकान्

याचको न तु याचकान् /



Between the crow and the

begger who is better?

The crow is better and not

the begger.

(When some food is offerred)

a crow invites other crows but

a begger does not do so.
