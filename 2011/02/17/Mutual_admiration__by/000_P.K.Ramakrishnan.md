उष्ट्राणां हि विवाहॆषु रासभा: खलु गायका: /

परस्परं प्रशंसंत: अहो रूपं अहो स्वन: //

  

In the marriage celebration of camels, donkeys were singers.

They were praising each other - what a figure! - what a voice!
