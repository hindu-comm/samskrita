-(कि)या जाना is a passivizing formation. Because संस्कृत has a direct  
कर्मणि (like passive) formation, the auxiliary verb (if needed) is अस्/  
भू  
स्त्रीणा गोभ्यस् तृणं दत्तम् (अस्ति/अभूत्/भविष्यति) । The अस्/भू form  
is pretty much always dropped if the tense is clear from the context.

But it would also be good to use the regular कर्मणि  
स्त्रीणा गोभ्यस् तृणं दीयते ।

Notice that the स्त्रीणा remains in the तृतीया विभक्ति.
