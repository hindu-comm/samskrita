Whatis called as periphrastic perfect appears to be whatShankaraji is looking for in Sanskrit. They are formed analytically with an auxiliary verb instead of by inflexion. Example: चोरयामास, ईक्षांचक्रे, . There is periphrastic future also, but that is a combination of nominative declension for third person and conjugation of verb अस् in first and second person.Ex: भविता-भवितारौ-भवितारः, भवितासि-भवितास्थः-भवितास्थ, भवितास्मि-भवितास्वः-भवितास्मः.
