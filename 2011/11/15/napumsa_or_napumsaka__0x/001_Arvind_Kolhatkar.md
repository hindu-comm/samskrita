I cannot say much about the question but it brought back to memory  
this long-forgotten shloka:

नपुंसक इति ज्ञात्वा प्रियायै प्रेषितं मनः।  
तत्तु तत्रैव रमते हताः पाणिनिना वयम्॥

Arvind Kolhatkar, Toronto, November 15, 2011.
