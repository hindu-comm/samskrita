For naming question, raised, both are equally coorect: either **Bhakta-rañjanī orBhakta-rañjinī and carry equal meaning: the one entertaining the bhakta-s.**

  

But**rajanī is the proper word and there is no alternative Sanskrit word interchangeable with it asrajinī meaning night.**
