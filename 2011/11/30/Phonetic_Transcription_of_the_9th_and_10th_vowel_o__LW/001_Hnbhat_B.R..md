Here is the diacritical roman transliteration for Sanskrit Vowels and consonants:

  

 \|\| ā ī ū ṝ ṛ ḹ ḷ ṅ ñ ṭ ḍ ṇ ẏ ś ṣ ṁ ɱ ḥ \|\|

  

for the following *devanāgarī*

  

॥ आ ई ऊ ॠ ऋ ॡ ऌ ङ् ञ् ट् ड् ण् य़् श् ष् ं ँ ः ॥

  

if you mean Roman Unicode IAST Transliteration encoding.

  

Others vary according to different software encodings to get*devanāgarī*characters by IME methods.
