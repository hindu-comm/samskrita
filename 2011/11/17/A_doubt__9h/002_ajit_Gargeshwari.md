द्वन्द्व सर्वपदार्थ प्रधानः In this the meanings of all the words are equally important. One is not dependent on another. When the compound is resolved all the members would be in the same case and connected by a conjunction.  
  
There are different types of द्वन्द्व samasa an example would be  
  
एकशेष: when several words of the same form or import are compounded only one is retained ex माता च पिता च = पितरौ
