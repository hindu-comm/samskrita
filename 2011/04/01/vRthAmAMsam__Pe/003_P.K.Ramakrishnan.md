I find the following in Manusmriti --

श्वाविधं शल्यकं गोधां खड्ग-कूर्म-शशांस्तथा /  
भक्ष्यान् पंचनखेष्वाहुरनुष्ट्रान् चैकतोदोदत: //

Manusmriti Chapter 5 Sloka 18

Meaning as per Apte's dictionary -  
श्वाविधं = porcupine  
शल्यकं = porcupine (probably a different variety)  
गोधा = alligater  
खड्ग = rhinocerous  
कूर्म = tortoise  
शश = rabbit

Here six are mentioned compared to five in Ramayana. Rhinocerous is the sixth.

I could not make out the meaning of the last line.
