Here is the complete verse:

  

प्रातः द्यूतप्रसंगेन मध्याह्ने स्त्रीप्रसंगतः ।  
रात्रौ चोरप्रसंगेन कालो गच्छति धीमताम् ॥

  

ascribed to चाणक्यनीतिदर्पणः (९. - ११)

  

[http://www.khapre.org/portal/url/sa/sahitya/pustak/chanakya/z81011112707(चाणक्यनीतिदर्पणाः.नवमोऽध्यायः).aspx](http://www.khapre.org/portal/url/sa/sahitya/pustak/chanakya/z81011112707(%E0%A4%9A%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%A3%E0%A4%95%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%80%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%A3%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%83.%E0%A4%A8%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%AE%E0%A5%8B%E0%A4%BD%E0%A4%A7%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%83).aspx)

  

The last line alone has found its own composition:

  

काव्यशास्त्रविनोदेन कालो गच्छति धीमताम् \|  
व्यसनेन तु मूर्खाणां निद्रया कलहेन वा \|\|

  

For the second version, there is a little change in the first पाद:  
वेदशास्त्रविचारेणकालो गच्छति धीमताम्\|  
the remaining being equal. SL Abhyankar has commented the second version, in his blog Sanskrit Learning:

  

<http://slabhyankar.wordpress.com/2010/10/16/learning-sanskrit-by-fresh-approach-lesson-64/>

  

There may be other readings too. I remember, reading as

  

काव्यालापविनोदेन for the first line. The source is not known.

  

The same difference as in the previous verse, बुभुक्षितैर्व्याकरणं न भुज्यते।
