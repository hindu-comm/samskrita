Namaskaarah

  

प्रातर्दूतप्रसंगेन

मध्याह्ने स्त्रीप्रसंगत: /  

रात्रौ चौर्यप्रसंगेन

  

कालॊ गच्छति धीमताम् //

  

It's meaning:

  

Read Mahabharata in the morning

Read Ramayana in the afternoon 

Read Bhagavatam in the night (the famous चोर = कृष्णः)

Time thus goes for the intelligent

  

Geeta
