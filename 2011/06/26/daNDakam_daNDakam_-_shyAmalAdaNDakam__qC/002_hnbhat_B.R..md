In continuation to my previous message, let me add comment on the 113, 114 .



  

After चण्डवृष्टिप्रपात which has got न-न- + ७ रगण-s,

the पाद-s with the increase of रगण-s form varieties of अर्ण, व्याल, जीमूत, लीलाकर, उद्दम, शङ्ख etc. i.e. thereafter gradually. i.e.

  

1.  चण्डवृष्टिप्रपातः which has got न-न- + ७ रगण-s 2.  अर्णः -न-न- + 8 रगण-s 3.  व्यालः =न-न- + 9 रगण-s; 4.  जीमूतः =न-न- + १० रगण-s 5.  लीलाकरः =न-न- + ११ रगण-s 6.  उद्दाम =न-न- + १२ रगण-s 7.  शङ्खः =न-न- + १३ रगण-s

This is meant by the comment of the editor. But without any limitation of numbers of र-गण-s, it is called in मत्तमातङ्गलीलाकर according to some other text on prosody..

  

But there is another variety increasing the यगण with



प्रचितकम् which has got न-न- + ७ यगण-s. Possibly this too may be increased.

  

A comment by the commentator, makes the increase of न-गण-s up to the number of वर्ण-s included in each पाद of दण्दक increased up to 999 by the addition of the गण-s of three letters. It goes practically give rise up to 1000 or more varieties.

  

एकोनसहस्राक्षरपर्यन्ता दण्डकाङ्घ्र्यः प्रोक्ताः।

वर्णत्रिकगणवृद्ध्या न द्वितीयाद्या महामतिभिः॥
