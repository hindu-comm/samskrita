काशिका 0n 1-4-32 कर्मणा यम् अभिप्रैति स सम्प्रदानम्  
कर्मणा करणभूतेन कर्ता यम् अभिप्रैति तत् कारकं सम्प्रदानसंज्ञं भवति। अन्वर्थसंज्ञाविज्ञानाद् ददातिकर्मणा इति विज्ञायते। उपाध्यायाय गां ददाति।



In view of the above explanation, the correct sentence will be:



सा एकस्मै पुत्राय जन्म दत्तवती if you want retain your structure.



Otherwise, सा पुत्रमेकं प्रासूत or सुष्वे। will be normal structure.



In all the case of the use of the verb दा = to give, the indirect object will take dative case.



And not that जन्मन् is the word for birth, which ends in "n" used in neuter gender as जन्म - जन्मनी - जन्मानि-

and not जन्मं



The same holds good in the other cases where the participles of दा is used in your story as highlighted by me. Please do the necessary corrections accordingly.
