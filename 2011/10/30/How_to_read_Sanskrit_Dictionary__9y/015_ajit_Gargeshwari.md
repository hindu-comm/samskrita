This word has no Vocative. It has the same form in all the genders. Has alternate forms in Accusative Dative and Genitive *cases.  
  
*These sentences mentioned below are grammatically correct  

तवं किं करोषि?  

अहम पाठं पठामि.  
युवाम किं कुरुथः ?  
अवाम पाठं पठावः  
यूयं किं कुरुथ?  
वयं पाठं पठामः
