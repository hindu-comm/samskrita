आगामि-शताब्द्याम् āgāmi-śatābdyām nextcentury  
अस्यां शताब्द्याम् asyāṁ śatābdyām inthiscentury

अब्दशत abdaśata century  
वर्षशतvarSaśata century

It seems to be a coined word translating century use in three genders irrespective of its derivation with grammar. If needed, it can be analogically defended on the analogy of पञ्चवटी, or त्रिफला, त्रिफली, as names. But it can be used in both in द्विगु compound once it is accepted.
