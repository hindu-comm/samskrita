I think Mallinatha should have quoted the lexicon in support of his lexicon.

  

स्वर्गेषु पशुवाग्वज-दिङ्-नेत्र-घृणि-भू-जले।

लक्ष्यदृष्ट्या स्त्रियां पुंसि गौः (३.३.२५) अमरः।

  

It is the word "गो" having different meanings and you will find if you look for the word in any dictionary any additional meanings than that given in Amara. It is the द्वितीया एकवचन of the word गाम् -

  

As in भगवद्गीता

  

गामाविश्य च भूतानिधारयाम्यहमोजसा ।  
पुष्णामि चौषधी: सर्वा: सोमो भूत्वा रसात्मक: ॥१५. १३॥

  

used in the same meaning.
