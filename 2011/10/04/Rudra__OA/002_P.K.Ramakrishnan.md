rudrah  

Sivapurana 6-19-14



rur duhkham duhkhahetum va

drvayati yah prabhuh

rudra ityuchyate tasmat sivah

paramakaranam



Meaning – rur is sorrow or the

reason for getting sorrow. The

lord who destroys that is rudrah

or siva.
