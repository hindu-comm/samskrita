झटिति प्रतिवक्तव्यं  
सभासु विजिगीषुभि: /  
अबद्धं वा सुबद्धं वा  
कुन्तीपुत्रो विनायक: //  
  
In an assembly if one  
wants to win, he should  
reply at once when  
questions are asked,  
not bothering whether  
it is correct or not.  
When asked to name  
the son of Kunti he  
should say Vinayaka.  
Immediacy is important.
