I have heard this samasya when I was a student of Sanskrit.

  

The answer is this -

  
वि: आट नगरे रम्ये

कीचकात् उपकीचकम् /

  

वि: = bird

आट = roamed about =flew

नगरे रम्ये = in the beutiful town

कीचकात्  = from one bambo

उपकीचकम् = to another bamboo
