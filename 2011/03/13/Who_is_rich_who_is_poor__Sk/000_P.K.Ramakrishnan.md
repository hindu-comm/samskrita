उपर्युपरि पश्यन्त: सर्व एव दरिद्रति /  
अधोध: पश्यत: कस्य महिमा नोपचीयते //  
  
Comparing with those who are wealthier  
everyone becomes poor.  
  
Comparing with those who are poorer will not  
  
everyone become wealthy ?
