A kuta-sloka.

One of the kuta-slokas  
I remeber is like this.

बर्हि बर्हिणवाजानां  
बर्हिणां बर्हिणामिव /  
पततां पततां घोष:  
पततां पततामिव //

I am unable to decipher  
the meaning. But the second  
part probably means -

The noice of the falling arrows  
is like that of the flying birds.
