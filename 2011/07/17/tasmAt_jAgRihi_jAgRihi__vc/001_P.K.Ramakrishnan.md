जाग्रतो जाग्रत

  

Some 70 years ago when I was staying

at Kaladi the birth place of Adi Sankara

a Sanyasi came visiting that place. For

any question addressed to him the reply

was always जाग्रतो जाग्रत.

  

-----------------------------------  
P.K.Ramakrishnan
