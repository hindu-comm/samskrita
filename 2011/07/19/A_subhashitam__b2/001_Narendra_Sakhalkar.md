Ayi Manyawaraha  
, Sadaram wande.  
A similar shloka is as follows:  
Yo na dadaati na bhunkte,sati wibhawe,naiva tasya taddrawyam.  
Trunamayakrutrimapurusho, rakshati sasyam, parasyarthe.  
Source:- Shaarangrava Paddhati 387.  
Ram,Ram.  
Narendra Sakhalkar
