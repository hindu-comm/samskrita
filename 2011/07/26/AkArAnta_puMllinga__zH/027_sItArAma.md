I am not denying that पा रक्षणे is a धातु । But when it joins with विश्व then resulting can not be धातु and therefore none of the सूत्र applicable to धातु should apply. \[धातु + प्रत्यय \] gets again धातु संज्ञा only when सनादि प्रत्यय follows. क्विप् does not fall under सनादि ।  

Just because विश्वपा has पा can not attract सूत्र pertaining to धातु।  

  

What is governing the joining of विश्व + पा ।  

  

पा + क्विप् = Entire - क्विप् gets लोप । क्विप् is in धातो: अधिकार - कृदतिङ् - makes it कृदन्त - therefore gets प्रातिपदिक संज्ञा based on
कृत्तद्धितसमासाश्च । 1-2-46

It became प्रातिपदिकम् Then सुँ आदि comes and entire derivation follows it is not a dhatu anymore. My question still remains how did आतो धातो apply.  

  

If you take पा only and attach क्विप् and convert it into कृदन्त & then प्रातिपदिक। Bring सुँ etc and then later do samas between विश्व + पा then also it is not धातु ।
