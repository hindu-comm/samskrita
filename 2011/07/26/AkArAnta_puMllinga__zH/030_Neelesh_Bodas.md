That is not correct. आतः is a विशेषण of धातोः, and it precisely means आकारान्तधातुः । This sutra isn't applicable for normalआकारान्त words likeहाहा. Kashika, kaumudi both are very explicit about this -आकारान्तो यो धातुस्तदन्तस्य भस्याऽङ्गस्य लोपः स्यात् . (see<http://ashtadhyayi.com/sutraani/6/4/140>)

  

Also note that this is in the scope of भाधिकार, so the complete meaning would be "आकारान्तधातोः भस्य अङ्गस्य आकारस्य लोपः भवति ।"

There is a siddhanta which says क्विबन्ताः विजन्ताश्च प्रातिपदिकत्वं न जहति, धातुत्वमपि न मुञ्चन्ति । That is, a विच्-प्रत्ययान्त word, though it is a pratipadikam, does not "throw away" the dhatu-संज्ञा. अतः "पा" इत्यस्य धातुसंज्ञा "विश्वपा" इत्यनेन अपि गृह्यते ।

  

Also understand that this is a special case where a pratipadik is retaining a dhatu-संज्ञा. And hence, it will undergo only those dhatu-karyas which are given specifically for a "प्रातिपदिक + धातु" hybrid words. And hence, तिङ् / कृत् etc pratyayas are not applicable for these words.
