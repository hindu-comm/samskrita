In Tamil, face is 'mukam' / 'mugam' (as far as the script goes, an ambiguous 'k/g' covers the entire non-nasal ka-varga of Sanskrit). A colloquial word is 'mUnji'.  

For mouth, the common word used is 'vAy'.

  

I am not sure if other options exist.
