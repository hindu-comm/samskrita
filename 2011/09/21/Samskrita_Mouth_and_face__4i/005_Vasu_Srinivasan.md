satyam brUyaat priyam brUyaat na brUyaat satyam apriyam \|  
priyam ca na anRutam brUyaat esha dharma: sanaatana: \|\|  
  
manu-smRuti (4:128)  
  
Truth must be spoken in a relished manner. Unrelished truth must not be spoken nor relished untruth. This is sanaatana dharma.
