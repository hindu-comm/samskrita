I could find many names with गणेशानी as surname on Googling. But I am reading an article on the female forms of गणेश popular in both तान्त्रिक and माङ्गलिक uses in the different traditions, not only Hindu, but also Buddhist sectarian cults in Tantric forms. One of them is interesting a form like अर्धनारीश्वर, गणेशानी is called kangithen, shothen, dishoetc. named in Chainese, female Ganapati embracing a male body, which is preyed and worshiped by lovers for the success of their love.

  

Source in a Kannada Magazie (Taranga).
