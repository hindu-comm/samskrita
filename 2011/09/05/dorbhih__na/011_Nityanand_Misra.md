चतुर्भिः दोर्भिः युक्ता - she who is endowed with four forearms  
  
दोर्भिः is the तृतीया बहुवचन form of "dos" (दोस्) which means forearm or arm.  
  
Apte gives the derivation as दम्यते अनेन इति दोस् (दम् + डोसि).
