The tortoise stone you are referring is not seen in the temples of Tamilnadu, only in Kerala

it is used for breaking the coconuts. The coconuts will be just thrown over it and it will break. It is similar to the "soorai thengai" breaking the coconut in front of Ganapathi into pieces.

here the effort for breaking it will be less.
