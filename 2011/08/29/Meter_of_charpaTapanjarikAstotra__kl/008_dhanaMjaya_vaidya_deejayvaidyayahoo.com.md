If the name of the meter from पिङ्गल is needed, it is (generally)  
पादाकुलक given by एभि: पादाकुलकम् \| ४.४७

There are 4 गणs, each with 4 मात्राs per line, and four lines per  
verse. Specific types of पादाकुलक are given in the prior sUtras 32-46.
