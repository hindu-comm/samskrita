The verse

न त्वहं कामये राज्यं न स्वर्गं नापुनर्भवम्।  
कामये दुःखतप्तानां प्राणिनामार्तिनाशनम्॥

occurs in भागवत at ix.21.12. I remember to have read that it also  
occurs in महाभारत. I cannot say which of these is the original source  
and which text is the borrower.

Arvind Kolhatkar, Toronto, August 25, 2011.
