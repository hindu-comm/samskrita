[[Nityanand Misra	2011-08-25, 19:00:03 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/LwsU7g1fU7w)]]



I do not desire heaven or liberation  
Nor freedom from transmigration  
I see beings burning with affliction  
I desire their suffering's destruciton
