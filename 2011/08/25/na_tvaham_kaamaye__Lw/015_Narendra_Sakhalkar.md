[[Narendra Sakhalkar	2011-08-26, 11:24:36 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/LwsU7g1fU7w)]]



Ayi Maanyaha,Sadaram Vande.  
The Shloka is from Bhabawat,[9.21.It](http://9.21.It) is included in the,'Uktivivesh,\[  
Page 144\] of Pandit Amerendra Gadgil,published by Vora and Co, Mumbai  
400 002,1976.  
It reads as follows:-  
Na twaham kamaye rajyam,na swargam,na punarbhawam.  
Kaamaye dukkhataptaanam praninaam aartinashanam.\[Bhagawat,9.21\]  
I sincerely regret my inability to render the couplet in the Devanagari script.  
\[I do not seek kingdom \[power\],nor heaven,nor another birth in this  
world.I wish to avert the pangs of the living beings, who are  
afflicted by sorrows.\]  
Ram,Ram.  
Narendra Sakhalkar
