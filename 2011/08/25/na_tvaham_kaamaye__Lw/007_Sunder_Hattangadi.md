[na](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/n/na) [kāmaye](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/k/kamaye) '[haḿ](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/h/ham) [gatim](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/g/gatim) [īśvarāt](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/i/isvarat) [parām](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/p/param)

aṣṭarddhi-[yuktām](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/y/yuktam) apunar-[bhavaḿ](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/b/bhavam) [vā](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/v/va)

[ārtiḿ](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/a/artim) [prapadye](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/p/prapadye) 'khila-[deha](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/d/deha)-[bhājām](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/b/bhajam)

[antaḥ](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/a/antah)-sthito [yena](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/y/yena) bhavanty [aduḥkhāḥ](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/a/aduhkhah)



[**SB 9.21.12**](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/neo/12/en): I do not pray to the Supreme Personality of Godhead for the eight perfections of mystic [yoga](http://us.mg4.mail.yahoo.com/y/yoga), nor for salvation from repeated birth and death. I want only to stay among all the living entities and suffer all distresses on their behalf, so that they may be freed from suffering.

``



न कामयेऽहं गतिमीश्वरात्परामष्टर्द्धियुक्तामपुनर्भवं वा ॥

आर्ति प्रपद्येऽखिलदेहभाजामन्तः स्थितो येन भवन्त्यदुःखाः ॥९–२१–१२॥
