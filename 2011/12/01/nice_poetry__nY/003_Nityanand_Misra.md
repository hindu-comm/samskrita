Magical work indeed - I have come across it earlier. Though this work is in विलोम, there are several other similar works in Shlesha श्लेष - I remember reading about Raghavapandaveeyam (राघवपाण्डवीयम्) where the verses narrate the life of Srirama and Yudhishthira. Another small work in Shlesha is Raghavamadhavashtakam or राघवमाधवाष्टकम् (attached) by Swami Rambhadracharya. This is from the recently published Sastipurti Abhinandan Granth which has many other of small works by the poet.  
  
Thanks, Nityanand
