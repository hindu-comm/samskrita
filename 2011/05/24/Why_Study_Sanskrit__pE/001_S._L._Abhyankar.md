Dear Mr. Daniel Tkach,  
  
Apart from philosophically rich scriptures like श्रीमद्भगवद्गीता and श्रीमद्भागवतम् beautiful सुभाषितानि also make an appealing aspect of learning संस्कृतम्  
I hope you have already known of my blog [http://slabhyankar.wordpress.com](http://slabhyankar.wordpress.com/), wherein I made an humble attempt to present a study of one सुभाषितम् at a time.  
You could quote the following सुभाषितम् in your pamphlet which, by itself explains, why to study संस्कृतम्  

भाषासु मुख्या मधुरा दिव्या गीर्वाण-भारती ।  
तस्माद्धि काव्यम् मधुरं तस्मादपि सुभाषितम् ।।  

When explaining तस्माद्धि काव्यम् मधुरं you may not fail to point out beauty of Sanskrit-poetry especially that of महाकवि-कालिदासः ।  
Learning संस्कृतम् can as well be a wholesome process of self-purification - purification of काया वाचा मनः !
