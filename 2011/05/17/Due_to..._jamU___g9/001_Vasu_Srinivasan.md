The word is ajnaanajam = a + jnaana + ja + m = not + knowledge + born + (2nd vibhakti suffix)

  

= yaH tamaH aj\~jnaanaat jaataH, tam

  

= Born out of ignorance (2nd vibhakti, objective case).
