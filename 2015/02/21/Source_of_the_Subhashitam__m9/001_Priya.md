This verse is quoted by Cowell in his preface to Sarvadarsanasangraha. He sources it as as being said by Yudhishthira in the Mahabharata iii. 17402. His version is a bit different -  
  
वेदा विभिन्नाः स्मृतयो विभिन्ना  
नासौ मुनिर्यस्य मतं न भिन्नम् \|  
धर्मस्य तत्त्वं निहितं गुहायां
