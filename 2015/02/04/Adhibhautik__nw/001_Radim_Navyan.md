[[Radim Navyan	2015-02-05, 13:49:40 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/nwb4vIiDpv8)]]



आधिभौतिक – suddenly an ice lays down the earth in the night. In the mourning you drive your car with no winter tires, so that one of the road-poles kisses your car while you making a high-speed-turn.  
आधिदैविक – you cut a cabbage, and at the time the evil spirit nudges your hand, you make a finger-cut.  
आध्यात्मिक – being mad, you imagine some things important for you, ant then whole life "follow the white rabbit" restlessly.  
  
उपसंहरामि–  
आधिभौतिकः – बाह्यः दृष्टः हेतुः यस्य, स तथा  
आधिदैविकः – अदृष्टः हेतुः यस्य, स तथा  
आध्यात्मिकः – आभ्यान्तरः स्व–मानसः हेतुः यस्य, स तथा  
  
इति एतत् मम मतः।
