नमस्ते श्रीमन् सालुदोस्-महोदय !

अभिनन्दनानि यद्भवान् संस्कृते लिखितुं प्रेरितः \| Congratulations, that you got this inspiration to compose something in Sanskrit !

  

Before commenting on the grammar and words chosen I think it is important to check what you wanted to say. I am hence making an attempt to put some translation of what you might have had in mind.

पूर्वं कारास्थपशुमिव अहं अभवम् = Earlier I was like an animal in bondage  
पूर्वं पूर्णसङ्कुलमनये अहं अवसम्= Earlier I stayed behind closed doors  
इदानीम् निर्मुक्तविहगमिव अहं उत्पतामि= Now I hop like a bird released  
इदानीम्विस्तारपक्षमेन अहं बोद्धामि = Now I feel like wings spread  

Is this translation okay ? Is this what you meant ?
