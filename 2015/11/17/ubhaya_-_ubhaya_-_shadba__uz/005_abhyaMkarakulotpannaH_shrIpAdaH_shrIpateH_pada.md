[[अभ्यंकरकुलोत्पन्नः श्रीपादः | श्रीपतेः पदयुगं स्मरणीयम् ।	2015-11-19, 09:17:33 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/uz7RXlJUQGo)]]



With reference toअष्टाध्यायी-सूत्रम्सर्वादीनिसर्वनामानि (१.१.२७) the detailing inगणपाठ lists following pronounsसर्व । विश्व । उभ । उभय । डतर । डतम । अन्य । अन्यतर। इतर। त्वत्। त्व। नेम। सम। सिम ।तद् । यद् । एतद् । इदम्। अदस्। एक। द्वि। युष्मद्। अस्मद्। भवत्। किम्।पूर्व \|अपरअवर दक्षिणउत्तर \|पर \|अधर \|स्व \|

(Ref. -[https://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/अष्टाध्यायी-गणपाठः](https://sa.wikisource.org/wiki/अष्टाध्यायी-गणपाठः)) This list obviously containsउभ and उभय. Being pronouns they have forms in all three genders. However in a bookरूपचन्द्रिका edited by Dr. Brahmanand Tripathi and published by Chaukhamba Surbharati Prakashan, Varanasi, it is mentioned thatउभ is only withद्विवचन andउभय is only singular and plural.

In GeetA (2-16)उभयोः also in (2-19)उभौ.

Although being pronouns they should have forms in all three genders, I wonder whether the forms will be identical in all three genders.
