नमस्ते श्रीमन्श्रीकण्ठ-महोदय !

  

अपि and किम् are also used as interrogatives. My [Lesson # 3](https://simplesanskrit.wordpress.com/category/lessons-1-to-10/lesson-3/) starts with use of किम् as interrogative. The best example of such usage are first two श्लोक-s in eighth chapter in गीता.

  

Also many words starting with क्-वर्ण such as कदा, कुत्र, कुतः, कथम्, किमर्थम् have interrogative sense inherent. Rather, interrogative sense can be taken as one characteristics ofक्-वर्ण.

  

सस्नेहम्  
अभ्यंकरकुलोत्पन्नः श्रीपादः ।  
"श्रीपतेः पदयुगं स्मरणीयम् ।"
