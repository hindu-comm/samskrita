\|\| Auṃ Hrīṃ ŚrīPārśvanāthāya Namaḥ \|\|

Jay Jinendra

  



जन्नत की चाभी है मेरी मुट्ठी में

अपनी माँ के पैर दबाया करता हूँ

  

\~ मुनव्वर राना

  

جنّت کی چابی ہے میری مٹھی میں

اپنی ماں کے پیر دبایا کرتا ہوں

\~ منوور رانا

  

jannat kii chaabhii hai merii mutthii mein

apanii ma ke pair dabaayaa kartaa huun

\~ Munavvar Rana

  

I have the key to heaven in my hands

I serve my mother with great reverence

  

  

Dear friends,

  

I wish you a very, very happy new year, full of love, luck, good health and great success. And the key to all these blessings - serve your parents with great love and devotion.

  

So let this year mark renewed efforts by all of us to give our parents the best and most comfortable lives that we possibly can.

  

Warm regards,

Manish Yashodhar Modi
