I am impressed by your question, seeking "... What अक्षर meansetymologically ..."

I would like to attempt अक्षर meansetymologically.

As a first step, I would look atअक्षर it as a compound word comprising of the components अ and क्षर. Of course अ is the negational component. Note, it is not a prefixउपसर्ग, because it is not listed in 22उपसर्ग-s detailed in Sanskrit grammar. अ is detailed at good length in Apte's Sanskrit-English online dictionary.

Coming back to अक्षर and to the second component क्षर,

I usually explore Apte's Sanskrit-English online dictionary for such queries of my own. For example forक्षर, I get this detail at <http://dsalsrv02.uchicago.edu/cgi-bin/philologic/contextualize.pl?p.2.apte.1051882>-

क्षर*a.*\[क्षरति स्यन्दते मुञ्चति वा, क्षर्-अच्\]**1**Melting away.**-2**Movable.**-3**Perishable; क्षरः सर्वाणि भूतानि कूटस्थो$ क्षर उच्यते Bg.15.16.**-रः**A cloud.**-रम् 1**Water.**-2**The body; ततः सवेदनः सद्यो जीवः प्रच्यवते क्षरात् Mb.14.17.17.**-3**Ignorance.**-4**The Supreme Being.**-5**Cause and effect.**-6**The natural form (प्रकृति); अक्षरक्षरयोरेव द्वयोः सम्बन्ध इष्यते Mb.12.35.1.  
  
From here, I can see thatक्षर is an adjective derived from the verbal rootक्षर् by affixing a suffix called asअच् in Sanskrit grammar. Use of this suffix is defined to beकर्मभावादौ to obtain adjectives and abstract nouns, denoting the action and sense implicit in the verbal root. It may be noted that hereक्षर is detailed first as adjective, then asक्षरःmasculine noun and also asक्षरम् neuter noun.

  

To understand "action and sense" implicit in the verbal rootक्षर् I get the following detail at<http://dsalsrv02.uchicago.edu/cgi-bin/philologic/contextualize.pl?p.2.apte.1051049>

क्षर् 1 P. \[क्षरति-क्षरित\] (Used transitively or in- transitively)**1**To flow, glide.**-2**To send or stream forth, pour out, emit; तेषु क्षरत्सु बहुधा मदवारिधाराः R.13.74; Bk.9.8.**-3**To drop, trickle, ooze.**-4**To waste away, wane, perish.**-5**To become useless, have no effect; यज्ञो$नृतेन क्षरति तपः क्षरति विस्मयात् Ms.4.237.**-6**To melt.**-7**To slip from, be deprived of (with abl.).*-Caus.*(क्षारयति-ते) To accuse, traduce (usually with आ). -With**वि**to melt away, dissolve.  

Finally I would summarize and relate all this study to understandअक्षर asयन्न क्षरति तदक्षरम् or यस्य क्षरः न तदक्षरः or यस्य क्षरं न तदक्षरम्
