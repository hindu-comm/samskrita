Learned friends,

  

I am looking for the source of the following Sanskrit quote:

  

काम! जानामि ते मूलं सङ्कल्पात् किल जायसे

न चाहं तं करिष्यामि यतस्त्वं भविष्यसि

  
kaama! jaanaami te muulam, sankalpaat kila jaayase

na chaaham tam karishyaami yatastvam bhavishyasi

  

O sensual desire! I know that you arise from thoughts.

But I shall not think about you. So you will not be able to come in existence.

  

Also, please let me know if the translation is correct. Thank you.
