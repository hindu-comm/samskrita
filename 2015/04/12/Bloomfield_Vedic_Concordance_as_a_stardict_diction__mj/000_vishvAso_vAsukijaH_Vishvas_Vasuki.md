How to get it/ install it?

  

[bloomfield-vedic-sa.tar.gz](https://github.com/vvasuki/stardict-sanskrit/raw/master/sa-head/tars/bloomfield-vedic-sa.tar.gz)isthe file.  

  

(How was this produced? Started with Nandu's <http://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_trial/upanishadwords/textfiles/concordancecorrect.txt> converted with Arun's <http://www.learnsanskrit.org/tools/sanscript> , followed by a LOT of regular expression based corrections, leading to[bloomfield-vedic-sa.tsv](https://github.com/sanskrit-coders/stardict-sanskrit/blob/master/sa-head/bloomfield-vedic-sa/bloomfield-vedic-sa.tsv "bloomfield-vedic-sa.tsv"), upon which the stardict tabfile tool was applied.)
