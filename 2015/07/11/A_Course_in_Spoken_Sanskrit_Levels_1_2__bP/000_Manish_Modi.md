Somaiya Centre for Lifelong Learning is happy to announce two courses in Spoken Sanskrit, Level 1 & Level 2. Each course will be for 6 months and it will cover grammar, conversation and the recital of some Sanskrit verses.  

  
Spoken Sanskrit Level 1:  

This is a beginners level course with an introduction to grammar and conversation.  
  
Course Schedule: 16 July - 24 December, 2015  

Timing: Every Thursday, 6.00 pm to 7.30 pm  
Fees: Rs 3000  
  
  

Spoken Sanskrit Level 2:  
Participants are expected to be familiar with basic sentence construction and present tense.  

  
Course Schedule: 15 July - to 23 December, 2015  
Time: Every Wednesday, 6.00 pm to 7.30 pm  
Fees: Rs 3000  
  
About the Facilitator:

The course will be conducted by Mr Ajay Pendse who has a Master's degree in Sanskrit (specialising in grammar) from Mumbai University as well as a Master's (Sanskrit Parangat) from Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth, Pune. He has received several awards, including the VN Mandlik Gold Medal from Mumbai University (in 2010) the TAV Dikshitar prize of Mumbai University (in 2011) for securing the first rank in Zala Vedant and the Shridhar Ramkrishna Bhandarkar Prize (in 2011) for securing the first rank in Sanskrit Vyakaranam. He is currently pursuing his PhD in Sanskrit from IIT Bombay.
