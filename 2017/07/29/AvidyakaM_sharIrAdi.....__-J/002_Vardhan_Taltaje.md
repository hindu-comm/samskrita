[[Vardhan Taltaje	2017-07-29, 19:44:18 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/-Jhu2yeZIOA)]]



The meaning given by P.N.Menon and Swamy Nikhilananda seem to be correct.  
  
आविद्यकं is derived from अविद्या (ignorance). A Taddhita pratyaya (ठक्) has been added to अविद्या to get the form आविद्यक. Since it is an adjective to दृश्यम्, it has taken the आविद्यकम् form.  
  
More learned persons may comment further.
