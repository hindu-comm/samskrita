[[Sunder Hattangadi	2017-07-24, 21:18:39 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/mjCxhEzzYCQ)]]



aap kevlamekmev shamayenrinaa himaanshustthaa  
dainya kalpkapaadpo divibhvh paap tathaa jaahnavee .  
saadhoonaamvaloknaat tryamid taapaadik yaasyti  
no teshaamupmaaasti kaaapi sadrishee loktrye kutrachit .  
āpaṁ kevalamekameva śamayenṝṇāṁ himāṁśustathā  
dainyaṁ kalpakapādapo divibhavaḥ pāpaṁ tathā jāhnavī \|  
sādhūnāmavalokanāt trayamidaṁ tāpādikaṁ yāsyati  
no teṣāmupamā'sti kā'pi sadṛśī lokatraye kutracit \|\|  
  
shree raman maharshi krit ॑ ulladu narpdu ॑ tAmiLa granthasya  
 anubnadh khandasya shreemaan lakshman sharmaa virchitoanuvaadh.  
śrī ramaṇa maharṣi kṛta ̍ ulladu narpadu ̍ tAmiLa granthasya  
 anubandha khaṇḍasya śrīmān lakṣmaṇa śarmā viracito'nuvādaḥ \|  
  
The moon can allay only heat for men;  
likewise the celestial wishing-tree can relieve only want;  
so too the (sacred river) Ganga can remove only sin;  
by the sight of sages all the three--heat and the rest--are removed;  
(so) there is nothing in all the three worlds comparable to them (Sages).  
 K.Lakshmana Sharma (aka 'Who') tr. of  
 Sri Ramana Hridayam (Revelation)  
 Anubandha verse 50
