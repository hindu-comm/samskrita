Namaskarams! A rare poem from Sri Hanumad Ramaayanam :

शिव शिरसि शिरान्शि यानिरेदुस्शिवशिव तानि लुटन्थि गृद्ध पादैहि \|

  
अयि खलु विषमह पुराकृतानां भवतिहि जन्तुषु कर्मनाम् विपाकह \|\|  

  

Shiva sirasi siraansi yaaniredussivasiva taani lutanthi gruddha paadaihi \|  
Ayi khalu vishamaha puraakruthaanaam bhavathihi jantushu karmanaam vipaakaha \|\|

  

source Telugu movie by name "Mahaakavi Kaalidasu"
