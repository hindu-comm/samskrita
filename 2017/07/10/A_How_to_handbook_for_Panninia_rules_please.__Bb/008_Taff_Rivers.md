Irene,  
  
 I have a hard copy of *The Tested Easiest Method of Learning & Teaching Sanskrit*, 7th Edition. 1982  
The original is 60 years old. The original and subsequent amended editions may well have been 'easiest' in them days.  
  
**However, be warned** that while it does *contain* English, each and every sentence has the Sanskrit purely in Devanagari.  
Not a single Sanskrit word is transliterated, not in the contents page not in the index pages.  
  
Thus it turns out to be completely useless to anyone such as myself who no more knows their their Devanagari than their Mandarin.  
  
Scanned or hand typed examples are hardly necessary for clarification.  
  
I shall follow up other suggestions that I am not familiar with.  
  
Thanks people,  
  
 Taff
