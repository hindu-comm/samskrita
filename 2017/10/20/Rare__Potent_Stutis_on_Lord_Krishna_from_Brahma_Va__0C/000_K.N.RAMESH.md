Courtesy: Sri.K.Muralidharan Iyengar  
  

Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. At the outset, may I extend a very Happy and Prosperous **Deepavali Festivities**!

  

In this connection, I am delighted to share 4 short, sweet and very potent stutis on **Lord Krishna** by **Lord Shiva**, **Lord Brahma**, **Lord Dharma** and **Goddess Sarasvati** - all taken from **Chapter 3** of **Brahma Vaivarta Puranam, Brahma Khanda**. Though the individual prayers are short, one can understand the potency of these stutis through the Phalashrutis given therein.

  

**May Lord Krisha bestow you/your family/kith and kin with everlasting happiness, health, wealth, fame, progeny and fruition of all your dharmic deeds and desires !**
