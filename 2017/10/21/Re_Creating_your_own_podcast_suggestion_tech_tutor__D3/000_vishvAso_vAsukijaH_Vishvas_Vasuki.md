> > **Why podcasts?**  
> > 
> > 
> > There are 3 major ways I and a HUGE number of people consume news: > Social media, TV/ YouTube and Audio Podcasts. I find that the last > avenue offers less than what I would like in two areas of particular > interest to me (hindu affairs and shaastra/ kaavya lecture-s). People > often find it far more convenient to listen to audio podcasts (say > while commuting or taking a walk) than sit in front of a computer or > TV outside of work. This is especially true in mobile-first countries > like India. Take a look at this old presentation > <https://www.slideshare.net/HubSpot/an-introduction-to-podcasting-47122931> > for numbers.  
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > Before wider broadcasting, I am targeting this initial mail > specifically to the BCC-ed people because I feel they might be > interested in filling this niche.  
> **  
> Publishing to a podcast stream is very easy** - see > <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FpwDrXkW1zo> . After some very simple > initial setup (described in the video description, but let me know if > you need any help) all one needs is a smartphone, an mp3 recording > app, and an internet connection. That's it. Then your latest audio > broadcasts will automatically be downloaded to the playlist of > subscribers (oft on their mobile phones) - ready to be listened when > the person has time (eg: commuting or exercising).
