I cannot answer this question.  
However, I wish to state the following mantra also occurs in the Tarpana

देवताभ्य: पितृब्यश्च महायोगिभ्य एव च। नम: स्वधायै स्वाहायै नित्यमेव नमो नम:॥  
I understand नम: is appropriate for obeisance to देवता:, स्वधा in case of पितृ and स्वाहा for addressing Yogis.  
R Subrahmanian
