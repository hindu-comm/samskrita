[[अभ्यंकरकुलोत्पन्नः श्रीपादः | श्रीपतेः पदयुगं स्मरणीयम् ।	2017-01-25, 16:14:29 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/JM5jNlrB0ow)]]



र् and ऋ are different from the point of view of phonetics, especially in deciphering meters of poetry. For example, in the word सुकृत the letterसु would have only one मात्रा. However in सुप्रभातthe letterसु would havetwo मात्रा-s. In सुकृत the letter कृ is not a conjoint consonant. It is simply a consonant completed by a vowel. In सुप्रभात the letter प्रis a conjoint consonant.
