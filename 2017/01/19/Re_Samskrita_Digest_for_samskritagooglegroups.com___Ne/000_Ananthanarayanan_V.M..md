It is not saarangapani, as is misspelt in Tamil. It is shArngapANi.
Refer vishnusahasranama - shArngadhanvaa gadhAdharah. rathANgapANirakshobhyah sarvapraharaNAyudhah.

Regards

Anantanarayanan
