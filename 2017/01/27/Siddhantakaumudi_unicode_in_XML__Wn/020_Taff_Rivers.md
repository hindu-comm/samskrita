The Siddhanta Kaumudi with English Translation.  
  
  
 For those interested, the source of the text for the 'XML Unicode project' with Devanagari and its commentary in English  
can be downloaded in book form (PDF etc.) from  
  
  
<https://archive.org/details/Siddhanta_Kaumudi_English_Translation-SC_Vasu>  
  
It is especially useful for Devanagari challenged English persons, such as myself.  
  
And it also has the Māheśvara Sūtras, which the 'project' omits.
