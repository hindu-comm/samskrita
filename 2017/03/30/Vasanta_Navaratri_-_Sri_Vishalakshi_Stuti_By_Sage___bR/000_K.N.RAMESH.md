Courtesy:Sri.K.Muralidharan Iyengar  

  
  

Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. As **Vasanta Navaratri** starts today, I am delighted to share a short and sweet prayer on **Sri. Vishalakshi** of **Kashi** by **Sage Vyasa** taken from **Saura Puranam and Chapter 8** titled **Trilochana Mahatmyam**.

  

The brief **Phalashruti** declares that one who duly takes bath in river **Ganga**, has darshan of **Sri. Vishalaskhi** and chants this hymn gets the benefit of performing **1000 Ashwamedha Yagas**.

  

May We Pray To **Sri Vishalakshi Devi** who manifests in all forms such as **Durga**, **Lakshmi** and **Saraswati**!

  

May I also wish a Very Happy New Yearwhich starts tomorrow for many of the Hindus who follow Lunar calendar! May The New Year Bring Eternal Joy in every endeavor!
