Ken, please go ahead and use whichever symbols you like when you write. Whether people understand what it is supposed to convey is another matter.  
  

As far as common English words such as bank etc., some Indian languages (e.g., Kannada) spell that as "byaank" - Kannada readers read that as bank without any confusion.  
  

There is nothing holy or unholy about scripts.
