Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste.

  

As **Sri. Vamana Jayanti** falls on **Saturday (02-Sep-2017)**, I am glad to attach a beautiful prayer by **Aditi** to **Lord Narayana** seeking the boon that **Lord Narayana** be born as her child (which was granted).

  

May we pray to **Lord Vamana** with this beautiful prayer on **Vamana Jayanti** day!
