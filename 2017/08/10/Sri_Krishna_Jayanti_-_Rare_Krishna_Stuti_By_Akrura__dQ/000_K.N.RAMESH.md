Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. **Sri Krishna Jayanti** falls on **11th August** (or 13th September depending on the traditions) and in that connection I am delighted to share a short and very sweet prayer on **Lord Krishna** by **Akrura** taken from **Padma Puranam**, **Uttara Khanda** and **Chapter 260** (titled **Kamsa Vadha**).

  

**Lord Shiva** mentions that one who chants this hymn at the time of taking bath gets the benefit of bathing in all holy rivers and attains **Lord Vishnu's** abode in addition to getting relief from all troubles.

  

May We Pray To Lord Krishna on **His Janmashtami day** with this exquisite prayer and accrue immense spiritual wealth!
