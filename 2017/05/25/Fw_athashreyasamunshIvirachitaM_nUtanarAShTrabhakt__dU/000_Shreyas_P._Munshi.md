सेवै भारतम्.= Let me serve Bharat,, त्रायै भारतम्,= Let me defend Bharat..सल्मै भारतम् = Let me pray for peace to be upon Bharat\|   
सल্म্ सल्मते  is a verb innovated by Shreyas Munshi from Urdu noun salaam. Placed in 1st Conjugation Atmanepada its third person singular (Prathama purusha ekavachanam) becomes "salm salmate" like "sev sevate"
