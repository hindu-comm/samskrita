Dr Narayan Gopal Dongare has studied extensively and written about 'Physics in ancient India'. Please see here-  
  
<http://sangamanee.com/Indian-Physics/Physics%20in%20Ancient%20India-20.htm>  
  

It is said here that he has found 'laws of motion' in Vaisheshik darshan.  
  

Two books written by him are-  
1) Physics in Ancient India
<https://books.google.co.in/books/about/Physics_in_Ancient_India.html?id=eNDaAAAAMAAJ>  
  
2) Kanada's Science of Physics
<https://books.google.co.in/books/about/Kanada_s_Science_of_Physics.html?id=E1vSygAACAAJ&redir_esc=y>
