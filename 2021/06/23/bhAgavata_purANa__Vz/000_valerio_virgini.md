सुप्तस्य विषयालोको ध्यायतो वा मनोरथः ।  
नानात्मकत्वाद्विफलस् तथा भेदात्मधीर्गुणैः ॥11.10.3॥

  

Dear scholars,  
could anyone explain what kind of compound is "भेद-आत्म-धीः"?

and the meaning of the phrase " भेद-आत्म-धीर् गुणैः "?  
  
thank you  

valerio

  

p.s.  
my idea is:  
"the understanding of आत्मन् is separate from the gunas"  

(where the instrumental गुणैः is used to indicate the thing from which one separates)
