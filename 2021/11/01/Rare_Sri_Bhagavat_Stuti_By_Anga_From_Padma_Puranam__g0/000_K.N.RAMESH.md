Dear All,  

  

Greetings and Namaste. As **Deepavali** festival is just a few days away, I am delighted to share a beautiful hymn on **Lord Krishna** by **Anga** taken from **Padma Puranam, Bhumi Khanda,** and **Chapter 32** titled **Venopakhyanam**.  

  

May I take this occasion to wish you, your family, kith and kin, and friends a **Very Happy Deepavali** and associated festivities. May the resplendent **Bhagavan** expunge all the darkness and bless you with health, wealth, prosperity, joy and consummation of all **Purusharthas**!  

  

May We Pray To **Bhagavan** with this beautiful prayer!

  

With best regards and Pranams,

K. Muralidharan Iyengar (Murali)
