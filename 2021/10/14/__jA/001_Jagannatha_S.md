Dear Mohan Chettoor,

  

In my view, fourth pāda must be इयुर्नश्वरसंपदः (इयुः = इण् गताविति धातोर्विधिलिङि प्र.ब.)

  

There is an opulence, depending on which, the individual soul gets spiritual bliss. How can the perishable opulence be equal to that supreme opulence?** **
