Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. As **Sri Rama Navami** falls on **21-Apr-2021** (Wednesday), I am delighted to share a rare 5-stanza stotram on Lord Rama by Lord Hanuman taken from **Srimad Devi Bhagavatam, Skandha 8,
and Chapter 20**.

  

In the brief **Phalashruti**, **Lord Narayana** declares that one who listens to the story of Lord Rama and the hymn gets absolved of all sins and attains the abode of Lord Rama.

  

May We Pray to Lord Rama with this hymn!

  

Thanks & Pranams,

K. Muralidharan Iyengar (Murali)
