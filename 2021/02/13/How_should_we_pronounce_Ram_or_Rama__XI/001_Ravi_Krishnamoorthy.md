For readers of Hindi, we are used to pronouncingराम as Ram. However, that is incorrect in Samskritam. The correct pronunciation is Rama. Ram would be written as राम् in Samskritam. Note the halant on the 'm' sound - this would be the correct way of eliminating the trailing अ sound in म.
