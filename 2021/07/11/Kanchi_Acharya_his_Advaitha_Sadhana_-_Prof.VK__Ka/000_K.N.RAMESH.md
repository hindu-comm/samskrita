The Kanchi Acharya, in his Advaita Sadhana, that was painstakingly translated by Prof V.K. ji and published in Advaitin forum years back, says: Shiva or Shakti leads the sadhaka to the ultimate realization and once that happens, erases itself. Vyavaharika satya comes to an end.
