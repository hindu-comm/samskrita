Dear list members  

  

This query is about*Mahābhārata* verse 12.111.24 (Critical edition 12.111.24).

  

The reading in the Gita Press edition (अनतीतोपधान्) is quite the opposite of the critical edition (तानतीतोपधान्). After a lot of thought, given the context, the critical edition reading makes more sense to me. I would like to know the version in other published editions: is it
अनतीतोपधान् or तानतीतोपधान्?

  

Critical edition (12.112.24)  
तानतीतोपधान् प्राज्ञान् हिते युक्तान् मनस्विनः  
पूजयेथा महाभागान् यथाचार्यान् यथा पितॄन्  
<http://gretil.sub.uni-goettingen.de/gretil/1_sanskr/2_epic/mbh/mbh_12_u.htm>  
  
Gita Press (12.111.24)  
अनतीतोपधान् प्राज्ञान् हिते युक्तान् मनस्विनः  
पूजयेथा महाभागान् यथाचार्यान् यथा पितॄन्  
<https://archive.org/details/mahabharat05ramauoft/page/4710/mode/1up>  
  
Commentary of Nilakantha (12.111.24)  
Same as Gita Press (though Nilakantha has not commented on this verse)  
<https://archive.org/details/mahabharata_nk/page/n2985/mode/1up>
