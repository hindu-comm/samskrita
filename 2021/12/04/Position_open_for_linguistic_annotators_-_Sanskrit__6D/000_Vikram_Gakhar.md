[[Vikram Gakhar	2021-12-04, 00:00:52 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/6DX2OfzTLX8)]]



Pratham Books has partnered with the[AI4Bharat Initiative](https://indicnlp.ai4bharat.org/)( <https://indicnlp.ai4bharat.org/> ) team on a couple of NLP projects, including the development of the input tools in Maithili and Konkani. They are now working on creating input tools across the scheduled languages of India and are looking for a Sanskrit resource (details below).



*The [AI4Bharat Initiative](https://indicnlp.ai4bharat.org/) in collaboration with [DesiCrew](https://desicrew.in/) *( <https://desicrew.in/> )* is looking for skilled linguistic annotators for a full-time position to perform data annotation tasks. The candidate must have extensive knowledge of grammar, sentence structure, understanding of parts of speech, and command of English and at least one of the 22 constitutionally recognized languages of India. The ideal candidate would have experience in linguistics, particularly syntax at the university level. This position calls for a motivated and meticulous data annotator who is both accurate and efficient. *
