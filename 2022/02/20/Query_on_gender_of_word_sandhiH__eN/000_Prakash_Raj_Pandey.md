Pranam to vidvans,

  
The word सन्धिः is used in masculine.  
  
We have this rule to guide us on matters relating to सन्धिः --  
  

संहितैकपदे नित्या नित्या धातूपसर्गयोः ।  
नित्या समासे वाक्ये तु सा विवक्षामपेक्षते ॥

  
Now what does -- सा -- and other feminine words refer to?
