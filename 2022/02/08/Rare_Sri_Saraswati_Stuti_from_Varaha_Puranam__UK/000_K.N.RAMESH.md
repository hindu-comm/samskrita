Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. As **Vasant Panchami** falls on **05-Feb-2022 (Saturday)**, I am delightedto share a very rare and sweet hymn on **Goddess Saraswati** by **Lord Brahm**a and retold by **Lord Varaha** in **Varaha Mahapuranam and Chapter 91**. **Lord Varaha** mentions that **Lord Shiva** made the **Goddess of Creation (Goddess Saraswati)** into 3 forms and **Lord Brahma**'s creation were made to grow only by Her grace.

  

I have also extracted the **namas** from this **stuti** and furnished the same as **Namavali** at the end (48 namas) - so that those who wish to perform **Archana to Goddess Saraswati** could use the same.

  

May We pray to **Goddess Saraswati** with this beautiful hymn!

  

With best regards & Pranams,

K. Muralidharan Iyengar (Murali)
