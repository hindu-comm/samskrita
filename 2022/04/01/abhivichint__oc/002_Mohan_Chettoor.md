##### Monier Williams Cologne

अभि-√ चिन्त् (impf. -अचिन्तयत्) to reflect on MBh. xiii, ४३४१.

  

##### Apte

विचिन्त् \[vicint\], १ U.

To think, consider.

To think of, ponder over, call to mind; विचिन्तयन्ती यमनन्यमानसा Ś.४. १.

##### Monier Williams Cologne

वि-√ चिन्त् P. Ā. -चिन्तयति, °ते, to perceive, discern, observe, KenUp.; to think of, reflect upon, ponder, consider, regard, mind, care for, MBh.; R. &c.; to find out, devise, investigate, MBh.; Pañcat.; to fancy, imagine, BhP.

  

To take into consideration, have regard to, regard; अस्मान् साधु विचिन्त्य संयमधनानुच्चैः कुलं चात्मनः Ś.४.१७.

To indend, fix upon, determine.

To devise, find out, discover.

To imagine.

To perceive, observe (Ved.).
