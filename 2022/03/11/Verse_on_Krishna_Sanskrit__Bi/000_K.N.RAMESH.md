काव्येऽस्मिन् यदि माधुर्यं तत्सर्वं त्वत्प्रसादत: ।



यत्किञ्चित् स्खलितं तत्र तत्सर्वं क्षम्यतां हरे ।।१४४९।।





O Krishna, if there is any sweetness in my poetry, it is all due to your

grace. Please pardon all my faults.





Madhav M. Deshpande

Professor Emeritus, Sanskrit and Linguistics

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA
