नमस्ते पण्डिताः

  

There is a famous statement:  

आत्मनः प्रतिकूलानि परेषां न समाचरेत्॥

I was told that the source is Mahaabhaarata, and I have found at least 3 different sites pointing to 5.15.17 as the source of this quote. But when I view the devanaagari this does not seem to be the case...

  

On the [sanskritdocuments.org](http://sanskritdocuments.org) site, there is the Critical Edition and the Southern Recension versions of Mahaabhaarata

In CE, the verse is :  

मयि क्रुद्धे जगन्न स्यान्मयि सर्वं प्रतिष्ठितम् \|  

देवदानवगन्धर्वाः किंनरोरगराक्षसाः \|\|१७\|\| (18 in SR)  

In SR it is  

न ह्यल्पवीर्यो भवति यो वाहान्कुरुते मुनीन् ।  

अहं तपस्वी बलवान्भूतभव्यभवत्प्रभुः ॥ 5-15-17 (16 in CE)  

  

When I searched more widely, I found the phrase in 3 different puranaani : Kurma, Padma and VishnuDharmottara. It is the full verse from the VishnuDharmottara 3.253.44 that seems to be quoted as being from Mahaabhaarata. This is:

तस्मात्सर्वप्रयत्नेन पापबाधां विवर्जयेत् ।।  
श्रूयतां धर्मसर्वस्वं श्रुत्वा चाप्यवधार्यताम् ।।  
आत्मनः प्रतिकूलानि परेषां न समाचरेत् ।। ४४ ।।
