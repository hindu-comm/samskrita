A MATTER OF HONOUR  
  
It is indeed a matter of great honour and pride that we have now begun stocking the publications of संस्कृत भारती Samkrita Bharati. At HGK we have prided ourselves on publishing the best possible books, and stocking the best publications in the world.   
  
For Sanskrit books, we have been selling the publications of Choukhamba, Rashtriya Sanskrit Sansthanam, Sampurnananda Vishvavidyalaya, etc. for several decades. But the prestigious publications of Samskrita Bharati were missing from our collection.   
  
We therefore made it a point to meet with Mr Satyanarayana Bhatta and Dr Sachin Kathale at Sanskrit Bharati’s Jnana Mandira at Bengaluru and thus began stocking their books.   
  

Let me begin by describing my favourites among their publications:  
  
भाषा प्रवेशः   
Bhasha Pravesh   
Set of 2 books  
2019  24 x 18 cm  416 pages Paperback  0.5 kilos  
₹260  
  
This set of textbooks is meant for those who participate in the excellent Sanskrit Sambhashana Shiviras organised by the Sanskrit Bharati. They also work as primers of Sanskrit for those who know the Devanagari script and wish to come to grips with Sanskrit. They are ideal for those who are learning Sanskrit in a classroom environment.   
  
We absolutely proud to stock these books at our bookstore. We ship books all over the world and accept payment by IMPS, NEFT, Paytm, Visa and MasterCard.
