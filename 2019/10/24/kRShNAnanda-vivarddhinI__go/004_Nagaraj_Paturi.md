The etymology of the word Radha is taken as coming from the same root as the root of aaraadhanaa. Thus Radha is viewed as the aaraadhyataa of Krishna.

  

Radha is the asvaadyataa of Krishna.

  

Radha is the rasyamaanataa of Krishna.

  

That is why she is the hlaadinee S'akti of Krishna.

  

Kali is being viewed as such a form in this name/description mentioned by you.
