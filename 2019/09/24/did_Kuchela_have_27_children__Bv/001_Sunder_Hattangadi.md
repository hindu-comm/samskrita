Bhaktha Kuchela (English: Devotee Kuchela) is a 1936 Indian devotional film in Tamil language directed and produced by lawyer turned filmmaker K. Subramaniam.\[1\] The film featured Papanasam Sivan as Kuchela and S. D. Subbulakshmi in dual roles as Lord Krishna and Kuchela's wife Susheela.\[2\]\[3\]

  

Plot

The film depicted the tale of friendship between the poor Brahmin Kuchela who had 27 children and Lord Krishna.
