Dear All,

  

Greetings and Namaste. As Sri Vinayaka Chaturthi falls on Monday, the 2nd September 2019, I am delighted to share a collection of 7 different short stutis on Lord Vinayaka taken from different chapters of Padma Puranam and Skanda Puranam. These beautiful hymns can be used as a collection or individually for daily prayers.

  

May Lord Vinayaka bless us all with prosperity in every dharmic endeavor in our lives.

  

With best regards and pranams,

  

K. Muralidharan Iyengar (Murali)
