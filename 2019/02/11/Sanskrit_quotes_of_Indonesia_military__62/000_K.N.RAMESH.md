Do you know a country which has motto of its Air Force, Infantry & Special Forces as....

  

\*'Karmanye Vadhikaraste Ma falesu Kadachana'\*,

A shloka from Bhagavad Gita.

  

Motto of its Navy is

\*"Jalasveva Jayamahe"\*

(victorious on the sea).

  

Motto of its Army is

\*"Kartika Eka Pakshi"\*

(Unmatched...bird with noble goals).

  

Motto of its Air Force is

\*"Swa Bhuavana Paksha"\*

(Wings of the motherland).

  

Motto of its Marine Corps is

\*"Jaleshu Bhumayamcha Jayamahe"\*

(Glorious on land and sea).

  

That country is Indonesia- the largest Muslim majority nation which prides itself on Hindu heritage.

It's National bird is - Garuda.
