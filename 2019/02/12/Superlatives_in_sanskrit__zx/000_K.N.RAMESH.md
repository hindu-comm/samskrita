Some Sanskrit words in superlative.

द्राघिष्ठ - longest

ह्रसिष्ठ - shortest

दविष्ठ - remotest

नेदिष्ठ - nearest

प्रथिष्ठ - widest

अणिष्ठ - minutest

गरिष्ठ - heaviest

लघिष्ठ - lightest

पीविष्ठ - fattest

तनिष्ठ - slimmest

PS: note that 'istha' in Sanskrit is 'est' in English.
