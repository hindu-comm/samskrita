Respected Rameshji,

  

A magnum opus Tamil translation of the Smriti Muktha Phalam has been carried out by Shri S.V. Radhakrishna Sastri in seven volumes and brought out by "Veda Dharma Sastra Paripaalana Sabha, Kanchipuram.
These books are available as PDFs in the link "<https://archive.org/details/S.V.RadhakrishnaSastriBooks>" (Sl. Nos.33 to 38 - Volumes 2 to 7). The first volume is available in the link <https://archive.org/details/SmrithimukthaphalamPart1>.

  

The translations for the shlokas referred in your question is available in Pages 470 & 471 (PDF file Page No.488 and 489) of Volume 2 (<https://archive.org/stream/S.V.RadhakrishnaSastriBooks/S.%20V.%20Radhakrishna%20Sastri/Sastra/Smrithimukthaphalam%20Part%202#page/n487/mode/2up>), a copy of which is pasted below for quick reference.
