प्रभुदयितसामन्तम् = प्रभुना दयितस्य सामन्तम् = neighborhood of those, who are blessed byप्रभु = सज्जनसङ्गतिः

-   Note, दय् 1 Ā. (दयते, दयित)= To bestow compassion upon, To bless
-   सामन्तम् = neighborhood  
-   प्रभुदयितसामन्तम् सेवस्व = Serve the neighborhood of those, who are
    blessed byप्रभु  

अतुलम् - can be taken as both (i) adjective of प्रभुदयितसामन्तम् (ii) adverb of सेवस्व

निष्काश्य - ल्यबन्तम् of निष्काश् whereकाश् 1, 4 Ā. (काश-श्य-ते, काशित)**1**To shine, look brilliant or beautiful

-   Henceनिष्काश्= To make without shine i.e. To make non-cognizable,
    To make non-manifest, To remove, To eliminate.  

The phrase is तां निष्काश्य. I am not clear of what or who that is, that is to be made निष्काशित.
