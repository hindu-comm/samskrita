[[valerio virgini	2019-02-04, 15:39:11 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/mN2dqvKxiOw)]]



the context of आरात् is the following:  

  

मनःशिक्षा  

  

रतिं गौरीलीले अपि तपति सौन्दर्यकिरणैः

शचीलक्ष्मीसत्याः परिभवति सौभाग्यवलनैः ।

वशीकारैश्चन्द्रावलिमुखनवीनव्रजसतीः

क्षिपत्याराद्या तां हरिदयितराधां भज मनः ॥१०॥

ie:  

क्षिपति आरात् या

  

Furthermore, there is an English translation attributed toश्री हरिदास शास्त्री बाबाजी  

  

*O mind! Immediately worship Srimati Radhika, the beloved of Sri Krsna who inflames Sri Ratidevi,*

*Sri Gauridevi, and Sri Liladevi by the rays of her beauty; who vanquishes Saci (Indrani),*

*Lakshmi and Satyabhama by her good fortune, i.e. by the abundance of her lover´s love;*

*and who dissipates the **pride** of the chaste young girls of Vraja, headed by Candravali,*

*by her ability to bring Sri Krishna under her control.*

  

Which seemed consistent withविस्मय  

  

Otherwise what sense can have आरात्in this verse?  

  

thanks

valerio
