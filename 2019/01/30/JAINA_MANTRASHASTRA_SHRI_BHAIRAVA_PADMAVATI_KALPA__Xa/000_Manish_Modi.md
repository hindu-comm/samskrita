JAINA MANTRASHASTRA

SHRI BHAIRAVA PADMAVATI KALPA

With Introduction, Comparative & Critical Study in English

-   Complete Texts of Several Jain Mantras in Sanskrit

\~ Mohanlal Jhavery & KV Abhyankar

2015 24 x 18 cm 644 pages Hardcover

Set of 2 books

₹4795

  

In this set of 2 books, the first volume in entirely in English. The second volume is entirely in Sanskrit.   
  
\*Volume I\* is a comparative and critical study of \*mantrashastra\* in general and Jain mantras in particular. It analyses \*mantravada\* and the \*tantric traditions\* found in Vedic, Jain, Buddhist and Zoroastrian texts.   
  
\*Volume II\* gives Jain mantras in their complete form. It commences with the \*Shri Bhairava Padmavati Kalpa\* of Acarya Mallishena Suri with a Sanskrit commentary. In addition, there are 31 appendices, which give Sanskrit texts of unknown Jain mantras such as   
\*AdbhutaPadmavati Kalpa, Padmavati Pujana, RaktaPadmavati Kalpa, Shri Parshvanatha Stotra, Padmavati Stotra, SarasvatiMantra Kalpa, Ambikashtakam, Cakreshvari Stotra, 64 Yogini Stotram, AnubhavaSiddhaMantra-Dvatrimshika\*, etc. concluding with the \*LaghuShanti Stava\* of Acarya Manadeva Suri.

  

This book also has several yantras and 12 colour plates of Jain goddesses.
