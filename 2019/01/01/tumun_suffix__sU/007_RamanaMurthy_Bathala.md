Thanks for correcting me in in case of भू

  

Here is the correct one

Step 1: Gunate radical vowel (इ/ई, उ/ऊ,ऋ/ॠ) in the root if possible

भू -> भॊ (Gunation possible)

  

Step 2: Add इ in case of सेट् roots

भो+ इ = भ् + ओ + इ = भ् + अव् + इ = भवि

  

Step 3: Add तुम्

भवि + तुम् = भवितुम्
