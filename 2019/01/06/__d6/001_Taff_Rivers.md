The Sanskrit indeclinable (avyaya):

  

I quote.

  

There are many words in Sanskrit that do not have multiple forms like nouns and verbs do.

Such words are called 'indeclinable'.

The majority of indeclinable words are either adverbs, conjunctions ('and', 'but', etc.) or particles such as the negative particle 'न' or particles used for emphasis like 'एव'.

  

Google for अव्यय in Sanskrit.
