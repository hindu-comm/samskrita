शयनम् is bed and at the ene of bahuvrivi it would mean one who is having bed such asभुजगशयन, पन्नगशयन, अनन्तशयन or कुसुमशयन. The word will not differ in meaning but its relation with the preceding word will be the same though the preceding word may change with or without change in meaning. The first three all the three preceding word has the same meaning, the last word is particular serpent named anantha. But in kusuma shayana also it means having the bed made of flowers. The bahuvrihi compound used as adjective it will take the gender to which it is related,

  

भुजग-शायिनम्,means one who lying usually in serpent.

शयानम् also means the same thing sleeping or lying. Both words are participles kridanta whereas in the first it a noun used for bed.
