"दशवर्षाणि च ताडयेत्"

  

This breaks the basic rule ofअनुष्टुभ्, viz., that eachपाद has 8 syllables. 'च' here is superfluous and can be safely dropped without hurting the meaning.

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
