हंस 1 . haṃsa, as, m. (according to Uṇādi-s.

III\. 62. fr. rt. 1. han)

the supreme Soul or universal Spirit (= brahman; according to Sāy. on Ṛg-vedaIV. 40. 5. in this sense derived either fr. rt. 1. han in the sense ‘to go,’ i. e. ‘who goes eternally,’ or resolvable into ahaṃ sa, ‘I am that,’ i. e. the supreme Being); the Jīvātman or living soul;

  

Atts. Sayana Bhasya on RV IV:40:5
