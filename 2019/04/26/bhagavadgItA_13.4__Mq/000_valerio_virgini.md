ऋषिभिर्बहुधा गीतंछन्दोभिर्विविधैः पृथक् ।  
ब्रह्मसूत्रपदैश्चैव हेतुमद्भिर्विनिश्चितैः ॥१३।४॥

  

Dear scholars,

  

the compound ब्रह्म-सूत्र-पद is normally translated and commented with reference to the वेदान्तसूत्र.

  

My question is:

  

Can it indicate a range of texts, both in पद and in सूत्र , concerning the ब्रह्मन्? and not a single work?

  

Can this be a grammatically correct translation?

  

thank you

valerio
