[[Nagaraj Paturi	2019-04-02, 00:00:02 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/v2E8fhDVmik)]]



Mrit + s'akaTika

  

Mrit = clay

  

s'akaTa = cart

  

-ika is a suffix meaning small version of

  

Thus

  

s'akaTika = little / toy cart
