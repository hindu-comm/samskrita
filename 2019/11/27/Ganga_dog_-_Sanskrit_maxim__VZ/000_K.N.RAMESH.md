``` का क्षतिरम्ब कटाक्षे न्यस्ते सति मयि विरिञ्चिवरपत्नि । गङ्गाशुनकन्यायान्महती मम वृद्धिरीरिता निपुणैः ॥ २४७॥ ```

[https://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_devii/shAradA300.html?lang=sa](https://www.google.com/url?q=https%3A%2F%2Fsanskritdocuments.org%2Fdoc_devii%2FshAradA300.html%3Flang%3Dsa&sa=D&sntz=1&usg=AFQjCNH9qA4mviGGb7lWffHs-ySSgZtXkw)

When the holy waters of Ganga come into contact with a dog, the waters are not polluted. On the other hand the dog gets rid of all its pollutions and impurities.
