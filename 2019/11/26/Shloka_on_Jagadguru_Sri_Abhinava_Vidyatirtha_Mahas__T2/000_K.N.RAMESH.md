Shloka on Jagadguru Sri Abhinava Vidyatirtha Mahaswamiji (Shishya) by Jagadguru Sri Chandrashekhara Bharati Mahaswamiji (Guru):  
  
दयासान्द्रं बालं निखिलजगतीरक्षणचणं  
  
हरन्तं विघ्नाद्रिं विमलविमलैर्वीक्षणचयैः ।  
  
परब्रह्माकारं प्रणवविदितं शान्तमनसं  
  
गुरुं विद्यातीर्थं कलयत बुधाश्चित्तकमले ॥  
  
dayāsāndraṁ bālaṁ nikhilajagatīrakṣaṇacaṇaṁ  
  
harantaṁ vighnādriṁ vimalavimalairvīkṣaṇacayaiḥ ।  
  
parabrahmākāraṁ praṇavaviditaṁ śāntamanasaṁ  
  
guruṁ vidyātīrthaṁ kalayata budhāścittakamale ॥  
  
O Wise people! Hold in the heart-lotus the Guru, Vidyatirtha, the young one with tranquil mind, who is exceedingly compassionate, who destroys the mountain of impediments by His extremely pure glances, who is skilled in protecting the whole world, who is known by means of the Praṇava, Om, and who is the manifestation of the Supreme Brahman
