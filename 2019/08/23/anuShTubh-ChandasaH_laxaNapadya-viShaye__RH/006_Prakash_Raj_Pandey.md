I found it difficult to place the following shloka in any defined category, certainly there are other categories.  
  
इत्थंभावेन कथितो भगवान्भगवत्तमैः ।

नेत्थंभावेन हि परं द्रष्टुमर्हन्ति सूरयः ॥

  

Bhagavat 2.10.44

  
The first quarter  र भ ल ग -- not defined

The second quarter स स ल ग -- मही

The third quarter र भ ल ग -- not defined

The fourth quarter र र ल ग -- not defined
