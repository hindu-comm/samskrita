Namaste,  
  
I think the following line from Bhagavad Gita 10.18 might fall into this category.  

भूयः कथय तृप्तिर्हि शृण्वतो नास्ति मे$मृतम्

  

There is also शृण्वतो नास्ति मे तृप्तिर्विचित्राणीह भाषसे ॥ in Vidura Niti and अहो जनसमूहेऽपि न द्वैतं पश्यतो मम। in Ashtavakra Gita.  
  
If one does a Google search with keywords like पश्यतो, शृण्वतो, etc, several instances from Puranic literature can be found.

  

  

regards
