[[Neelesh Bodas	2019-12-12, 08:34:31 [Source](https://groups.google.com/g/samskrita/c/2fa7ygH0qAo)]]



नमस्ते,

  

This is the ध्यानश्लोक of महासरस्वती. In the last two lines of this श्लोक, the सरस्वती is characterized by four adjectives -

\(1\) गौरीदेहसमुद्भवा - Born from the body of Gauri, See Chapter 5 of देवीसप्तशती for details.

2\) त्रिजगताम् आधारभूता - Supporter of the three words

3\) महापूर्वा - The one whose name gets the prefix "महा" (Essentially, महासरस्वती).

4\) शुम्भादिदैत्यार्दिनीम् - The slayer of शुम्भ and other daemons (धूम्रलोचन, चण्ड, मुण्ड, रक्तबीज, शुम्भ, निशुम्भ and thousands of others. शुम्भ was the king of them, hence "शुम्भादि")
