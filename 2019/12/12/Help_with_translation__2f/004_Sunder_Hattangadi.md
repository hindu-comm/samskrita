शुंभ śumbha m (S The name of an Asura or demon slain by Durgá.) Applied, appellatively, to a dull, sluggish, and stupid fellow.

  

सुंभ sumbha m ( H) A miser or niggard. 2 (Vulgar for शुंभ) The name of an असुर or demon. Hence, appellatively, a sluggish and stupid fellow.
