Theफलश्रुतिः of the कनकधारास्तोत्रम् is:

  

स्तुवन्ति ये स्तुतिभिरमूभिरन्वहम्

  

त्रयीमयीं त्रिभुवनमातरं रमाम् \|

  

रमा गुणाधिका गुरु·तरभाग्यभागिनो

  

भवन्ति ते भुवि बुधभाविताशयाः \|\| २१ \|\|

  

The translations I've seen for भवन्ति ते भुवि बुधभाविताशयाः are something like:  

  

"they will gain a powerful intellect which will earn praise even from the learned".

  

But is this different translation grammatically possible.

  

भाविता can mean "in the future" andआशय can mean "accummulated karma not yet manifested".  

  

Canबुधभाविताशयाः mean "future accummulated karmas are known"

  

and the phraseभवन्ति ते भुवि बुधभाविताशयाः mean "they will know their future karmas in this world (i.e. in this very lifetime) ?
