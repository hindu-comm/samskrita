 अभिप्रायश्छन्द आशय इति नामलिङ्गानुशासनम् 3.2.20  

Their intellectuality/views (आशय )will be respected (भावित) by the learned ones (बुध).

Intended meaning: They will have name and fame in the circle of scholars/ they will reach high level in scholarly thinking.
