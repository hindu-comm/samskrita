यथा मां बाधते शीतं बाधते माधवं तथा ।  
पण्ढर्यां पाण्डुरङ्गस्य शिशिरे कर्णबन्धनम् ।।९२९।।  
  
Just as the cold affects me, so does it affect Krishna. Look at the  
ear-covering of Vitthala in Pandharpur during the cold season.
