Thank you Ajay,

  

Some versions (including the one I have) have: iṣṭāviśiṣṭamatayo instead of iṣṭā viśiṣṭamatayo i.e.:

iṣṭāviśiṣṭamatayo 'pi yayā dayārdradṛṣṭyā

triviṣṭapapadaṁ sulabhaṁ labhante \|

  

And a translation I have interpretsiṣṭāviśiṣṭamatayo asiṣṭa-aviśiṣṭamatayo and

translates the phrase as:

Her compassionate glance enables those who are dear to her as well as those who lack discrimination to reach the abode of the gods with ease.

Thanks,

Harry Spier
