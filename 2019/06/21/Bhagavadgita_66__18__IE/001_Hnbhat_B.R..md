In the first मा शुचः it is aurist लुङ् due to the operation of Panian rule माङि लुङ् and न माङ्योगे. Without मा it would be अशुचः।

  

The second form is from a verb derived from the noun मोक्ष with णिच् suffix लृट् of मोक्षयामि - मोक्षयिष्यामि।

  

I hope it is clear.
