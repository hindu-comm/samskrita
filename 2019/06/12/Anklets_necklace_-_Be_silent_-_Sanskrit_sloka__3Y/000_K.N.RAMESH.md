मौखर्यं लाघवकरं मौनमुन्नतिकारकं ।  
मुखरं नुपूरं पादे कण्ठे हारो विराजते ॥

Talkativeness leads to disrespect, silence leads to prosperity (is  
golden ),an anklet which makes noise is worn on the feet while a necklace  
adorns the neck.
