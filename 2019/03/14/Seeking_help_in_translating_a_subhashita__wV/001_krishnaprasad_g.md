This is a passive construction.

A garland is प्रत्यक्षेपि( put)by sita in Rama's neck.

प्रत्यक्षेपि = प्र + क्षिप्=to throw + कर्मणि +लुङ् =aorist. 3rd person singular

Allकर्मणि +लुङ् formations are similar and easy

भवति अभावि
