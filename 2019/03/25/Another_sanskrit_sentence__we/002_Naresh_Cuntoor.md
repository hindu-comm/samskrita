> > My questions are: >
> 1) for "with great force" can you say महाप्रयत्नेन instead of महता > प्रयत्नेन >
>   
> > 
> > 

  

Yes. Both mean the same. The vyasta prayoga ( महता प्रयत्नेन ) sounds better than the samasta prayoga ( महाप्रयत्नेन ) though.  



> 
> > 
> > 2) for "opened with great force" can you just say महता प्रयत्नेन > विवरेण or महाप्रयत्नेन विवरेण instead of महता प्रयत्नेन कृतेन विवरेण >
>   
> > 
> > 

  

No. Changes the meaning here.  

The sense is this -  

महता प्रयत्नेन यः विवरः कृतः तेन गृहं प्रविश्य ….  



> 
> > 
> > 3) for "opened" can you say अपहृत्य instead of आहृत्य . >
>   
> > 
> > 

  

Not "opened", but "stolen". Either word works.
