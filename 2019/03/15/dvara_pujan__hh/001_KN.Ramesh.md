This is done by us during Kumbha mangalvar... ( Maasi chevvai in tamil)..

There is no outlined procedure for this..

Only thing is one performing this pooja has to observe

1\. mauna from getting up & till the pooja is over..

2\. No house member should cross the doorstep except the performee

3\. The performee should not cross the doorstep in between the pooja.( She has to carry everything outside before starting the pooja)

4\. Only sumangalis are permitted to do this
