It is split as you do for English words, but it will not give you the intended meaning.

  

In the 1st, they are split into two words. param =but, aatmaa = soul. Two are unrelated and make no sense.

  

In the second, it is considered as one compound word and has to be split in accordance to the grammatical rules of compound formation into two conjugated words as

  

परमः आत्मा \> परमात्मा = the suprme soul

  

as कर्मधारय compound and not by splitting with hyphenation.
