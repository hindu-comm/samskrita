It is from हिंस् root and not from हन्। The translation doesn't relate to none of the verbs. The word by word translation by the commentator? न not? हिनस्ति destroys? आत्मना by the self? आत्मानम् the Self? ततः then?? न not? हिनस्ति destroys? आत्मना by the self? आत्मानम् the Self? ततः then?**Swami Sivananda**

  

न not हिनस्ति destroys आत्मना by the self आत्मानम् the Self.

  

Translation:

  

13.29 Because he who sees the same Lord eally dwelling everywhere does not destroy the Self by the self; he goes to the highest goal
