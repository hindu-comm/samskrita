There is no word itihasam, but इति+ह+आस = thus it probably happened.

  

Please refer to any dictionary: Here is the Meaning in English:

  

इतिहास

  

(H3) इति--हा\* स \[p= 1320,3\] \[L=28773.1\] see below

(H3) इति-हा\* स \[p= 165,2\] \[L=28785\] m. (इति-ह-आस , " so indeed it was ") , talk , legend , tradition , history , traditional accounts of former events , heroic history S3Br. MBh. Mn. &c

  

Here is meaning in Sanskrit:

  

itihāsa

  

\[L=4099\] \[p= 1-205\] *itihāsaḥ, puṁ*, (itiha āste'smin . itiha

āsa ghañ .) pūbbavṛttāntaḥ . pācīnakathā .

tatparyyāyaḥ . purāvṛttaḥ 2 . ityamaraḥ ..

(yathā, manuḥ . 3 . 232 .

“ākhyānānītihāsāṁśca purāṇāni khilāni ca” .)

vyāsādipraṇītabhāratādigranthaḥ . iti bharataḥ .
