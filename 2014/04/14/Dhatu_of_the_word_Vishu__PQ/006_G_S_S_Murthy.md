In Sanskrit-Kannada dictionary of Chakravarti Srinivasagopalachrya, विषु, विषुव and विषुवत् are listed. Meaning for "viShuva"\[ neuter gender\] is given as "an instant when night and day are equal" \[my translation into English\]. It is also given as follows: }विषु साम्यमस्यास्ति वः (वा ५-२-१०९)
