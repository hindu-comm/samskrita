SATI+API= SATYAPI(yan sandhi)

TY**A** is not long , but short.



SATI is saptamee ekavachana form of SAT.

API is avyaya



In Samskrta, it is called Bhaavalakshanaa saptamee or Satsaptamee.



In English, locative absoulute.



Translation: O lord ! in spite of having unity./ lack difference/absence of duality, Ibelong to you (you have authority over me). But you do not belong to me(I have not authority over you).
