आपटे-वर्याणां शब्दकोशे विष-शब्दस्य व्युत्पत्तिः \[विष्-क\] एवं विवृता अस्ति \| तेन प्रायः विष-शब्दः विष्-धातुतः \|  
विष्धातुरपि अस्ति विवृतः <http://dsalsrv02.uchicago.edu/cgi-bin/philologic/contextualize.pl?p.5.apte.1605609> अत्र निम्नमिव -  
विष् I. 3 U. (वेवष्टि, वेविष्टे, विष्ट) **1** To surround. **-2** To spread through, extend, pervade. **-3** To embrace. **-4** To accomplish, effect, perform. **-5** To eat. **-6** To go to, go against, encounter; (not generally used in classical literature). -II. 9 P. (विष्णाति) To separate, disjoin. -III. 1 P. (वेषति) To sprinkle, pour out.
