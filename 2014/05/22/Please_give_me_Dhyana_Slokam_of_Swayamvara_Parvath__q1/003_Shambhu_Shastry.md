Aum asya shree svayamvara paarvatee mahaa mantrasya

aja RiSiH, panktiH ChandaH svayamvara paarvatee devataa \|

  

Then kara nyaasa followed by aNga nyaasa:

  

aum jaga thraya vashya mohinnai,anguSTaabhyaam namaH \| hr\`dayaaya namaH \|

hreem trailokya vashya mohinnai, tarjaneebhyaam namaH \|shirase swaahaa \|  

hroom uraga vashya mohinnai, madhyamaabhyaam namaH \| shikhaayai vaSat \|  

hraim sarva raaja vashya mohinnai, anaamikaabhyaam namaH \| kavachaaya hum \|

hraum sarva stree puruSa vashya mohinnai, kaniSTikaabhyaam namaH \| netratrayaaya vauSat \|

hraH sarva vashya mohinnai \| kara tala kara pr\`STaabhyaam namaH \| astraaya phat \|

  

Day/morning dyaana shloka:

  

baalaarkaayuta suprabhaam karatale lolamba maalaakulaam \|

moolaam sandadheem manohara tanum mandasmitodyanmukheem \|\|

mandam mandamupeyusheem varayitum shambhum jaganmohineem \|

vande deva muneendra vandita padaam iStaarthadaam paarvateem \|\|

  

Evening/night dhyaana shloka:

  

karadhr\`ta vanamaalya sarva ratnaaNga bhooSaa

nikhila nayana chetohaari roopaagrya veshaa \|\|

bhavatu bhavadabheeSta praaptaye shaila kanyaa

puruSa yuvati vashyaakr\`Sti nitya pragalbhaa \|\|

  

Aum hreem yogini yougoni............aakarSaya namaH (svaahaa).
