Totally irrelevant though, I am tempted to quote a parody on tarkika phraseology:

श्यामा कुट्टिरयं साधुः कुट्टित्वात् पूनिकुट्टिवत्  
पाम्बु कुट्टौ अतिव्याप्तिः तद्भिन्नत्वं निवेश्यते ॥

There are a few Tamil words: pUnikuTTI=kitten, paambukuTTi=baby snake, ShyaamaakuTTI is the name of a person.
