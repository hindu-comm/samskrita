I was looking for a kannada verse using sanskrit vRutta. Here is one taken from "kannada kaipidi".

ಆಕಾಶಾನ್ತಮನೆಯ್ದೆ ನೀಳ್ದ ವಿಕಟಾ\*ಟ್ಟಾಲಂಗಳೊಳ್ ಕೇತನಾ\*-

ನೀಕಂಗಳ್ ---". This is Sardulaavikridita and yati should have occurred where I have put a star.
