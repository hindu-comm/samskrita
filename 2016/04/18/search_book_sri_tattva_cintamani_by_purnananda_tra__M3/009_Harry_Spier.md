The complete Sritattvacintamani by Purnananda (in Sanskrit) is in the Muktabodha digital library and is downloadable for free. There is an on-line searchable version in transliteration and a downloadable devanagari version in pdf.  

  

Go to [www.muktabodha.org](http://www.muktabodha.org) and then click on the digital library link. A page will come up with links to our three sanskrit digital libraries. The text is in "Searchable E-text Library" click on this link and follow the instructions and you can then view and dowload the text.
