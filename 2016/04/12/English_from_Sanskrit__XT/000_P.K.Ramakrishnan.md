aham = ah + am Take away am. It becomes ah = i

  

vayam = vi + ayam Take away ayam. Then it becomes vi or we.

  

tvam = tu + am. take away am. It becomes tu = thou

  

yuyam = yu + am. Take away am. It becomes yu or you.

  

saha  thau  they (=they)

  

thad = that
