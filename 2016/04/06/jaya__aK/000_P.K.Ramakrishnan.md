Narayanam namaskritya

Naram chaiva narottamam

Deveem sarasvateem vyaasam

Tato jayam udeerayet

  

This is the first sloka in Mahabharata.

  

Jaya means victory.

  

According to katapaydi jaya refers to 18.

ja - 8 ya - 1 ankaanaam vaamato gatih. So it means 18

  

Mahabharatha has 18 parvas.

The Mahabharata war lasted 18 days.

It was fought by 18 akshohinis - units of fighters.

7 of Pandavas and 11 of Kauravas.

Bhagavadgita is part of Mahabharata - it has 18 adhyayas.
