\> The टाप् prescribed by भागुरि relates to generally to हलन्त feminine gender words like

॥वष्टि भागुरिरल्लोपमवाप्योरुपसर्गयोः।  
आपं चैव हलन्तानां यथा वाचा निशा दिशा॥८

like वाक् - वाचा, निट् - निशा, दिक् - दिशा.

And it does not make कर्मन् feminine gender कर्मा, नामन् - नामा. It does not change the gender by टाप्, as seen from the example both are feminine gender.

This case of डाप् is specially for अन्नन्त in बहुव्रीहि compound, as ङीप् prescribed for नान्त is prohibited in अन्नन्त बहुव्रीहि and outside the compound कर्मन् is never used in feminine gender. How and why it should take टाप्?
