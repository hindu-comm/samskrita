> > Greetings and Namaste. The Hindu solar new year (**sauramAna > samvatsara**) and **Sri Rama Navami** fall on two consecutive days > (**April 14th/15th, 2016**) and in this connection I am glad to attach > a short prayer on **Lord Rama** by **Devas** **taken from Padma > Puranam, Patala Khanda, Chapter 5**. **Lord Rama** mentions that one > who chants this hymn twice a day sees fear from enemies, poverty, > disease eliminated resulting in happy life. >
> 
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > 
> > May I take this occasion to wish you, your family, kith and kin a very > Happy and Prosperous **Durmukhi** Year! May The New year herald an era > of endless prosperity, happiness, achievement and fulfillment in all > walks of your lives. At a timethe world is challenged by extremism, > terrorism, religious intolerance and all forms of divisive forces, it > is the bounden duty of the followers of Sanatana Dharma to follow the > scriptures and lead by example - **sarve janAH sukhino bhavantu** (May > all people live happily and comfortably). >
> 
> > 
> >   
> > 
> > 
> > Incidentally, North East India, Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, etc. also > celebrate their new year next week (April 13th/14th) - which is known > as **Songkran** - a word which originated from **sankrAnti** > (Sanskrit) - passage of sun from one sign to another. Similar to Holi, > drenching people with water is a symbolic activity during Songkran to > signify washing away of impurities and sins and purifying oneself. In > Indonesia/Bali, there are Tirthas for purification as holy waters in > India (**Tirtha Gangga**, **Tirtha Empul**) as below >
