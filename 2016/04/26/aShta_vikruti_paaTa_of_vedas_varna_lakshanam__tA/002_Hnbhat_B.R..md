\> Want to know the meaning of क्रमपूर्वा मनीषिभिः  
\>  

प्रोक्ताः = said  
मनीषिभिः = by wise scholars  
क्रमपूर्वाः = in order or based on the order (of the words)  
विकृतयः = recitation models based on change of word orders
