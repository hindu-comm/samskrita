\> Are they then declined like रमा ?  
\>

In बहुव्रीहि compound, feminine gender,

१. ० सीमा - सीमानौ -- सीमानः - declined like सीमन्

२. ० सीमा - सीमे - सीमाः - declined like रमा

३.. ०सीम्नी - ०सीम्न्यौ - ०सीम्न्यः - declined like नदी

All the others are also declined in three ways. But दामन् preceded by numerals, will be declined in third way like नदी - त्रिदाम्नी - ०दाम्न्यौ - ०दाम्न्यः only.
