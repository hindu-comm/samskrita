Once again I am here with a question from what I read in Shukraniti:

  

कालमानं त्रिधा ज्ञेयं चान्द्रं सौरं च सावनम् ।

भृतिदाने सदा सौरं चान्द्रं कौसीदवृद्धिषु ।

कल्पयेत् सावनं नित्यं दिनभृत्येऽवधौ सदा ॥ २-३९१ ॥

Now I do not understand what is 'Savan' calendar or time scale.

  

Monier-Williams offered this:

  

N. of Varuṇa;  
a month of thirty solar days; a particular kind of  
day; a natural or civil day from sunrise to sunset; a  
particular kind of year (as distinguished from the  
solar year)  

  

Can anyone share more information, especially the scale or length of Savan year and how it is different from Solar or Lunar calendars?
