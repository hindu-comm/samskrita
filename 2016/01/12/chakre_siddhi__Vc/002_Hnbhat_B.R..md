\(1\) कृ + लिँट् । By[3-2-115](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:3-2-115)परोक्षे लिँट्,

\(2\) कृ + ल् । अनुबन्ध-लोपः by[1-3-2](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-3-2)उपदेशेऽजनुनासिक इत्,[1-3-3](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-3-3)हलन्त्यम्,[1-3-9](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-3-9)तस्य लोपः।

\(3\) कृ + त ।[3-4-78](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:3-4-78)तिप्तस्झिसिप्थस्थमिब्वस्मस्तातांझथासाथांध्वमिड्वहिमहिङ्mandates the प्रत्ययः “त” as the substitute for the लकारः। By[3-4-115](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:3-4-115)लिट् च, a तिङ्-प्रत्यय: which comes in place of लिँट् gets the आर्धधातुक-सञ्ज्ञा।

\(4\) कृ + एश् । By[3-4-81](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:3-4-81)लिटस्तझयोरेशिरेच्, when they come in place of लिँट्, the affixes “त” and “झ” take the substitutions “एश्” and “इरेच्” respectively.  
As per[1-3-10](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-3-10)यथासंख्यमनुदेशः समानाम्,[1-1-55](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-1-55)अनेकाल् शित् ो, “एश्” comes as a substitute for “त”।

\(5\) कृ + ए । अनुबन्ध-लोपः by[1-3-3](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-3-3)हलन्त्यम्,[1-3-9](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-3-9)तस्य लोपः।  
Note: As per[1-2-5](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-2-5)असंयोगाल्लिट् कित्, the “ए”-प्रत्यय: is a कित् and hence[1-1-5](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-1-5)ग्क्ङिति चstops[7-3-84](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:7-3-84)सार्वधातुकार्धधातुकयोः।

\(6\) कृ कृ + ए । By[6-1-8](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-8)लिटि धातोरनभ्यासस्य, when लिँट् follows a verbal root, there is reduplication of the first portion – containing a single vowel – of the verbal root which is not already reduplicated. But if the verbal root (that has more than one vowel) begins with a vowel, then the reduplication is of the second portion – containing a single vowel.

Note: As per[1-1-59](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-1-59)द्विर्वचनेऽचि, while reduplication is yet to be done, a substitution (in this case रेफादेश: by[6-1-77](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-77)इको यणचि) shall not be made in the place of a vowel on the basis of a vowel (in this case एकार:) that is the cause for reduplication.  
Note: This rule only temporarily stops the substitution until reduplication is done. Once reduplication is done, the substitution does takes place (in step 10 below.)

Therefore we apply[6-1-8](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-8)लिटि धातोरनभ्यासस्यbefore applying[6-1-77](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-77)इको यणचि।

\(7\) कर् कृ + ए । By[7-4-66](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:7-4-66)उरत्‌, a ऋवर्ण: of the अभ्यास: (reduplicate) takes the अकारादेश: when a प्रत्यय: follows.  
By[1-1-51](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-1-51)उरण् रपरः, in the place of ऋवर्ण: if an अण् letter (“अ”, “इ”, “उ”) comes as a substitute, it is always followed by a रँ (“र्”, “ल्”) letter.

\(8\) चर् कृ + ए । By[7-4-62](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:7-4-62)कुहोश्चुः, in a reduplicate (अभ्यासः – ref.[6-1-4](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-4)पूर्वोऽभ्यासः), a letter of the कवर्गः or हकारः is replaced by a letter of the चवर्गः । The substitutions take place as per[1-1-50](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:1-1-50)स्थानेऽन्तरतमः।

\(9\) च कृ + ए । By[7-4-60](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:7-4-60)हलादिः शेषः, of the consonants of a reduplicate (अभ्यासः – ref.[6-1-4](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-4)पूर्वोऽभ्यासः), only the one (if any) at the beginning is retained, the rest are elided.

\(10\) चक्रे । By[6-1-77](http://avg-sanskrit.org/avgdocs/doku.php?id=sutras:6-1-77)इको यणचि।
