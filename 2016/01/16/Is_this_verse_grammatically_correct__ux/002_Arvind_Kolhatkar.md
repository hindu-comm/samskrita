Harry,

  

I found the following at<http://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_shiva/bilvaashhtaka.html?lang=hi> in the stotra calledबिल्वाष्टक :

``` लक्ष्म्यास्तनुत उत्पन्नं महादेवस्य च प्रियम् । बिल्ववृक्षं प्रयच्छामि ह्येकबिल्वं शिवार्पणम् ॥ ६॥ ```

This makes perfect sense. Your Roman version  

lakṣmyāḥ stanata utpannaṁ mahādevasya ca priyam bilva-vṛkṣaṁ prayacchāmi hyeka-bilvaṁ śivārpaṇam \|\|creates the problem . The correct text is not 'laksmyah stanata' but 'laksnyastanuta' ' or, in Devanagari,लक्ष्म्यास्तनुत. लक्ष्म्यास्तनुत उत्पन्नं means 'created from the body of LakSmI'

Arvind Kolhatkar
