चेतन खेले होरी

\~ कविवर द्यानतराय

  

चेतन खेले होरी

सत्य भूमि, छिमा बसन्तमें

समता प्राण प्रिया संग गोरी

चेतन खेले होरी  

  

मन को माट, प्रेम को पानी

तामें करुनाकेसर घोरी

ज्ञान-ध्यान पिचकारी भरी-भरि

आप में छारैंहोरा-होरी

चेतन खेले होरी  

  

गुरु के वचन मृदंग बजत हैं

नय दोनों डफ लाल कटोरी

संजम अतर विमल व्रत चोवा

भाव गुलाल भरैं भर झोरी  

चेतन खेले होरी

  

धरम मिठाई तप बहु मेवा

समरस आनन्द अमल कटोरी

द्यानत सुमति कहे सखियनसों

चिर जीवो यह जुग-जुग जोरी

चेतन खेले होरी  

  

  

CETANA KHELE HORĪ

\~ Dyānatarāya

  

cetana khele horī

satya bhūmi, chimā basaṃta meṃ

samatā prāṇa priyā saṃga gorī

cetana khele horī

  

mana ko māṭa, prema ko pānī

tāmeṃ karunā kesara ghorī

jñāna-dhyāna picakārī bharī-bhari

āpa meṃ chāraiṃ horā-horī

cetana khele horī

  

guru ke vacana mṛdaṃga bajata haiṃ

naya donoṃ ḍapha lāla kaṭorī

saṃjama atara vimala vrata covā

bhāva gulāla bharaiṃ bhara jhorī

cetana khele horī

  

dharama miṭhāī tapa bahu mevā

samarasa ānanda amala kaṭorī

dyānata sumati kahe sakhiyana soṃ

cira jīvo yaha juga-juga jorī

cetana khele horī

  

  

**CONSCIOUSNESS PLAYS HOLI**

\~Kavi Dyanatray

  

On the ground of Truthfulness,  

In the spring of Forgiveness,  

The Soul plays Holi

With its most deeply loved friend, Equanimity.

Consciousness plays Holi.  

  

In the earthen pitcher of the Mind,

It carries the water of Love,  

Mixed with the saffron of Compassion.

In it, it fills the water pistols of Knowledge and Meditation

And it aims them at itself.

Consciousness plays Holi.

  

Like the sound of the mridanga drum,

The Teachings of the preceptor are heardclearly.

Both Points of View\* reverberate like a tambourine.

Restraint is like perfume.

Flawlessly Followed Vows keep the soul on the right path.

True Disposition of the soul is the gulaal\*\* which is scattered by the bagful.

Consciousness plays Holi.

  

Dharma is the sweetmeat, Penance the dry fruit.

The bliss of Equanimity renders the Receptacle flawless.

O Dyanat, Wisdom tells her friends,

May the pair of the Soul and Equanimity remain together forever!

Consciousness plays Holi

  

  

NB This poem is a fine allegory based on the Indian festival of Holi, depicting a deeply spiritual aspect to this joyous occasion. The poet has skilfully imbued the normal paraphernalia of playing Holi with spiritual significance. For him, the earthen pot signifies the mind and the soul is the fair maiden playing Holi.

  

\*naya = point of view

\*\*gulaal = coloured powder thrown at each other during Holi
