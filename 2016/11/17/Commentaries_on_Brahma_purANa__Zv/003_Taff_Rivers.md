Further tothe troublesome***tridaivatyā*of**the**Madhva** school philosophy and his indulgence in a little poetic prose.

  

  

 **Madhva** associated the ***three*** strands of **prakṛti**, ***sattva***, ***rajas*** and ***tamas***, with the ***three*** female deities, ***Śrī***, ***Bhū*** and ***Durgā***.

  

 Thus easily expressed as **daiva**, and in the sense of **derived from**, as in the vocabulary of the time\*.

  

 \* **daiva**

   mf(ī)n. (fr. deva) belonging to or ***coming from*** the gods, divine, celestial, AV. Br. Mn. MBh. &c. \[**497,2**\]
