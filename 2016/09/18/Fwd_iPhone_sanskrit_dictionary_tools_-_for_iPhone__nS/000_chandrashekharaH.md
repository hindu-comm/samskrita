In the link above, you'll find a few pieces of sanskrit related  
software tools. Most importantly:  
  
(1) mudgala Kosha - This is a windows app which will allow offline  
searches of the Monier Williams dictionary. It also integrates with a  
buch of web tools (Apte dictionary, INRIA reader, JNU declension  
engine etc). Installation instructions & screenshots are available  
from <http://www.aupasana.com/software/kosha>  
  
(2) stardict instructions for the iPhone and android. This allows  
android users access to the monier williams skt-eng dictionary on  
their phone. iPhone users have it even better -- they can also do  
eng->skt searches. Android instructions and screenshots are available  
from <http://www.aupasana.com/software/stardict-android> , and iPhone  
instructions and screenshots are available from  
<http://www.aupasana.com/software/stardict>
