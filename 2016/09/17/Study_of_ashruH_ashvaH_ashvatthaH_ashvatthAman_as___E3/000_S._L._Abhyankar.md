नमांसि सर्वेभ्यः !

  

When doing word-by-word study of गीता, including study of etymology of the word, I came across the words अश्रुपूर्णाकुलेक्षणम् 2’1, अश्वानाम् 10’27, अश्वत्थः 10’26 अश्वत्थामा 1’8. Some interesting commonalities started revealing. It came to mind that these words – [अश्रु, अश्व, अश्वत्थः, अश्वत्थामन्](https://dhaatusingeetaa.wordpress.com/category/%E0%A4%B6%E0%A4%AC%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A6%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AD%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%B8%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%83/%E0%A4%85%E0%A4%B6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%81%E0%A4%83-%E0%A4%85%E0%A4%B6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%83-%E0%A4%85%E0%A4%B6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%A4%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%A5%E0%A4%83-%E0%A4%85%E0%A4%B6/) – make an interesting group of words and hence merit their study as a study of an interesting group of words.

  

These words make an interesting group, because their etymologies are interesting. They are curiously interlinked even by sounds वर्ण-s inherent in them.

  

I hope, you will also like a Venn diagram there !

  

सस्नेहम्
