# Saraswati Gayathri Mantra

***Saraswati Gayathri Mantra***



Benefit (s) : Goddess Saraswathy - the consort of Lord Brahma, is the goddess of education, knowledge, arts, music, and sculptures. Recitation of Saraswathi Gayathri Mantra improves one's education and knowledge.



Best Time to Chant : *Brahma muhurta*, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays



Number of Times to Chant : 9, 11, 108, or 1008 times



Aum Vaak-dhevyai Cha Vidmahe

Virinchi Pathniyai Cha Dhimahee

Thanno Vaanee Prachodayath





***Bharathi Dhevee Gayathri Mantra***



Benefit (s) : This mantra bestows one with the knowledge to write songs, poetry, stories, and literature. Also improves both the verbal and non-verbal communications skills.



Best Time to Chant : *Brahma muhurta*, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays



Number of Times to Chant : 9, 11, 108, or 1008 times



Aum Naakaaraayai Cha Vidmahe

Maha Vidyaayai Dhimahee

Thanno Bharathi Prachodayath





***Vani Dhevee Gayathri Mantra***



Benefit (s) : The Vani Dhevee Gayathri mantra helps one to improve in education and excel in examinations. It also helps to gain awards, honours, and recognitions.



Best Time to Chant : *Brahma muhurta*, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays



Number of Times to Chant : 9, 11, 108, or 1008 times



Aum Naathamaayai Cha Vidmahe

Veena-tharaayai Dhimahee

Thanno Vani Prachodayath
