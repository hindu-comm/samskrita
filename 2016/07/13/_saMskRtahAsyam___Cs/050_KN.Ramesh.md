Idli uses one ladle of batter.

Dosa also uses one ladle of batter.

But the cost of Idli is Rs.10/-

Cost of Dosa is Rs.40/-

Why?

Idli has small circle where as Dosa has a larger circle.

  

hence instead of having a narrow mind, let us have a broad mind..

Then our value will improve.
