\> स्व-घोषं निमज्जन्तम् आख्यापयन्तम् ।

\> जितत्वं = jita+tva (formation of abstract noun)or jita+tvam (you)?  
\>

Certainly it is the first जितत्वं in the accusative, object of आख्यापयन्तम् declaring (yourself) won by the devotees to those know his omniscient nature.

\> शतावृत्ति = it is an avyaya or a compond?

It is an adverbial compound शतावृत्ति वन्दे qualifying the verb meaning "I bow to him one hundred times" with love.
