Dear Group,

  

I have a problem with the correct wording of verse 11.32 of theभगवद्गीता. At several places, for example at

\< <http://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_giitaa/bhagvadnew.html?lang=sa> \>

and at

\< <http://www.bhagavad-gita.org/Gita/verse-11-30.html> \>

  

the words of verse 11.32 are

  

कालोऽस्मि लोकक्षयकृत्प्रवृद्धो लोकान्समाहर्तुमिह प्रवृत्तः ।  

ऋतेऽपि त्वां न भविष्यन्ति सर्वे येऽवस्थिताः प्रत्यनीकेषु योधाः ॥ ११-३२॥

  

The words ऋतेऽपि त्वां न भविष्यन्ति सर्वे\`\` have been translated as
\<Even without you, none will be spared>

  

The wordऋत has the meaning of \`the truth\`. How ऋतेऽपि त्वां can mean \`even without you\` baffles me. I wonder whether the correct word should be हृतेऽपि, asहृत means \`without, bereft of\`.

  

Arvind Kolhatkar.
