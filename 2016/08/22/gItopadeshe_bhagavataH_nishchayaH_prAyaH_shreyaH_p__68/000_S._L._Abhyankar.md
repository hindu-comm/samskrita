नमांसि सर्वेभ्यः !

गीतामुपदिशन्श्रीकृष्णभगवान् wished not just that Arjuna be motivated back to fight the battle, but also that Arjuna should understand the highest or most sublime truth.

With this thought, I attempted compilation of verses dwelling on[श्रेयः परमवाप्स्यथ](https://study1geetaa2sanskrit.wordpress.com/category/thematic-studies-of-%E0%A4%97%E0%A5%80%E0%A4%A4%E0%A4%BE/%E0%A4%B6%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B0%E0%A5%87%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%83-%E0%A4%AA%E0%A4%B0%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%B5%E0%A4%BE%E0%A4%AA%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%B8%E0%A5%8D%E0%A4%AF%E0%A4%A5/).

Hope, you would like it. Comments are solicited most earnestly.  

सस्नेहम्  
अभ्यंकरकुलोत्पन्नः श्रीपादः ।  
"श्रीपतेः पदयुगं स्मरणीयम् ।"
