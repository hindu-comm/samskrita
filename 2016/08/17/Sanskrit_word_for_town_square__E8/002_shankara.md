Namaste,

  

Another word for square or junction is चत्वरम्.  

  

From Monier Williams' Dictionary - चत्वरम् n. rarely m. (\[Hariv. 6499 ff, R.V. v, 49, 15\]) a quadrangular place, place in which many ways meet, cross-way MBh. &c  
  
From Apte's Dictionary - चत्वरं \[L=13066\] \[p= 0484-a\] 
\[चत्-ष्वरच् Uṇ. 2. 121\]  
A place where many roads meet; स खलु श्रेष्ठिचत्वरे निवसति Mrcchakatikam 2. — 3  



regards
