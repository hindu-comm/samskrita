\>  
\> How is word मन्युः formed? Apte's dictionary gives मन् + युच्. How does this word get the meaning from the verb root मन्?  
\>

It is formed by उणादिसूत्र ३.२०. यजिमनिशुन्धिदसिजनिभ्यो युच् ॥ यज्युरध्वर्यु:। मन्युर्दैन्ये क्रतौ क्रुधि । शुन्ध्युरग्नि: । दस्युस्तस्कर: । जन्यु: शरीरी । with युच् suffix in the three meanings along with other roots.

The word does not mean only anger, but sorrow, sacrifice क्रतु also as per Amara. Why the question about the meaning 'anger' only?

*मन्युर्दैन्ये*क्रतौ क्रुधि । । ३.३.६६८ । अमर also records the three meaning.
