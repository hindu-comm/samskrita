नमस्ते प्रसन्ना-महोदये !  
दृश्यते शमिका-शब्दस्य व्युत्पत्तिः प्रायः शमिन्-शब्दात् \|  

It seems that the word शमिका has its etymology from word शमिन्.  

शमिन्-शब्दः आपटे-महाभागस्य शब्दकोशे एवं विवृतोऽस्ति \|  

This word शमिन् is detailed in Apte's dictionary as below -  

![Inline images 1](https://groups.google.com/group/samskrita/attach/bdb11257c2714ee2/%E0%A4%B6%E0%A4%AE%E0%A4%BF%E0%A4%A8%E0%A5%8D.jpg?part=0.1)  

तेन शमिका = calm, tranquil, peaceful, self-controlled.  
  

I wish that your शमिका also grows to become a girl of such qualities and you will have a jolly good time with her.

  

सस्नेहम्  
अभ्यंकरकुलोत्पन्नः श्रीपादः ।  
"श्रीपतेः पदयुगं स्मरणीयम् ।"
