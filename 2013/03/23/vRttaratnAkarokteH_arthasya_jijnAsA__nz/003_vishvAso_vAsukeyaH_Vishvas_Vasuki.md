मुख्यतया एवम् उक्तम्-

1.  A syllable with
    an*[anusvara](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anusvara "Anusvara")*('ṃ')
    or
    a*[visarga](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Visarga "Visarga")*('ḥ')
    is always*guru*. 2.  All other syllables are*guru*, either because the vowel
    is**dīrgha**("long"), or because the*hrasva*vowel is followed by
    a consonant cluster. 3.  the last syllable in each foot may be considered**guru** 4.  **लघुचिह्नम् । (ऋजु), गुरुचिह्नम् ऽ (वक्रम्)**
