The answer is Paraskara and Katyayana are the same person. The Grihya Sutra is called Paraskara Grihya Sutra and Shrauta Sutra is called Katyayana Shrauta Sutra. Names may have been interchanged by writers. Another example is Hiranyakeshin and Satyashada being names of the same person.
