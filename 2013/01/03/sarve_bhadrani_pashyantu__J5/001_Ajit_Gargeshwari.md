This is a Shanti mantra that is recited to bring peace and Harmony to all. The context is clear.  
  
Here is a discussion on this topic in another list  
  
<https://groups.google.com/forum/#!topic/bvparishat/mTIwMgCDm6g/discussion>  
  
  
<https://groups.google.com/d/msg/bvparishat/mTIwMgCDm6g/-Ki8v8Ni1KkJ>  
  

ॐ सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः सर्वे सन्तु निरामयः।

सर्वे भद्रणिपश्यन्तु मा कश्चिद्दुःख भाग भवेत्॥

  
The source for the Shanti Mantra is not clear some guesses  
Brihadaaranyaka Upanishad 1.4.14 or from Atharva Veda XIX:ix:1-14  
  
Regards  
Ajit gargeshwari
