य र ल व श ष स ह

1 2 3 4 5 6 7 8



In case of combined letters the

last letter is to be used.

Independent vowels are all zeros.



In Carnatic music this is used to indicate

the rank of melakarta raga. For example

harapriya is the name of the raga. Khara

meaning 22 is added to mean that it is the

22^(nd) raga. So it is called kharaharapriya.



Naraayana Bhattathiri compoased the

Narayaniyam in honor of Narayana of

Guruvayur in more than thousand verses.

The last verse of this is –



अज्‍ञात्वा ते मह्त्वं यदिह निगतिदं विश्वनाथ क्षमेथा:

स्तोत्रं चैतत् सहस्रोत्तरमधिकतरं त्वत् प्रसादाय भूयात् /

द्वेधा नारयणीयं श्रुतिषु च जनुषा स्तुत्यतावर्णनेन

स्फीतं लीलावतारैरिहमिह कुरुताताम् अयुरारोग्यसौख्यम् //



The last word not only prays for ayuh, arogyam

and saukhyam but also indicates the kalidinasamkhya

1712210 when he completed the stotram.

This 172210 comes from the katapayaadi.

One has to read in the reverse order.
