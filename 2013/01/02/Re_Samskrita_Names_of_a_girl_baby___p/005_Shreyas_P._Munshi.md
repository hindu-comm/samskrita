Anaghaa is a good name. Not too long...not to short...means 'sinless'. Another name is vihaa  
MW विहा \[L=203166\] ind. = स्वर्ग , heaven Un2. iv , 36 Sch. 'aa' ending indeclinable; I have heard it as a small girl's name.  
Personally I think the name should be such that it does not need abbreviation; it should be easy to pronounce; not so scholarly sounding that it gets mispronounced. Although common and not fancy and fashionable the two syllable names like Uma, Sheela, Neha etc are good.
