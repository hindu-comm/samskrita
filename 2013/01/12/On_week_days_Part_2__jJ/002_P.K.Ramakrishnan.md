Ancients have arranged the order according to the time taken by each body

to complete one cycle in the zodiac. These are

  

Sani 30 years

Guru 12 years

Kuja 625 days

Surya 365 days

Sukra 225 days

Budha 88 days

Indu 27 days

  

All the above are as viewed from the earth.
