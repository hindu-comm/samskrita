When प्रणतः क्लेशनाशाय , it will mean : I (have) bow(ed) down to kR^iShNa to destroy torments.  

When प्रणतक्लेशनाशाय , it will mean : I (have) prostrate(d) to kR^iShNa who is destroyer/destruction of torments.  

Both are correct.
