One cannot apply affixes randomly from any प्रकृति. I already said that 4-3-119 तस्येदम् ordains अण् as a तद्धित suffix. तद्धित suffixes are under the अशिकार of 4-1-1 ङ्याप्प्रातिपदिकात् which runs through entire fourth and fifth chapters. चुर् a root is neither ङी nor आप् nor प्रातिपदिक so अण् cannot be applied directly to चुर्. To summarize it simply "Apply कृत् suffixes to roots and तद्धित to words formed from roots".  
  

Secondly अण् will cause आदिवृद्धि (उ -> औ, as in बुद्ध/बुद्धि + अण् = बौद्ध), and भलोप (elision of final syllable of the प्रकृति).  

  

Finally, all affixes carry a meaning. The meaning of "of X" is totally different from "like X".
