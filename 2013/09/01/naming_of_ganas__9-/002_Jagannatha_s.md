If the query is on names of *da**śagaṇī* of *Vy**ākaraṇa*, the following *saṅgrahakārikā* may be quoted :

*bhvādyadādī juhotyādir divādiḥ svādireva ca/*

*tudādiśca rudādisca tanakryādicurādayah //*

The following *saṅgrahak**ārikā* of mine mentions *vikara**ṇa*s of *ga**ṇa*s.

*bhū-śap, lug-ad, juhoti-ślu,*

*śyan-div, su-śnu, tudādi-śa/*

*rudādi-śnam tan-u, krī-śnā*

*cur-ṇic+śap gaṇavikriyāh //*

**(*asandeh**āya kvacit sandhir na kṛtaḥ*)
