**2)** For the verse -  
स्वधर्ममपि चावेक्ष्य । वर्णाः ८  
न विकम्पितुमर्हसि । वर्णाः ८  
धर्म्याद्धि युद्धाच्छ्रेयोऽन्यत् । वर्णाः ८  
क्षत्रियस्य न विद्यते ।। वर्णाः ८  
Following analysis of the meter is at <http://study1geetaa2sanskrit.wordpress.com/2011/10/02/learning-together-sanskrit-and-geeta-chapter-2-verse-31-32-%E2%80%93-post-44/>  
  

अत्र अनुष्टुभ् छन्दः ।  
Analysis of अनुष्टुभ् छन्दः is to be done by checking conformance only of 5th, 6th and 7th letter in each line.  
  
अस्य लक्षणपदम् – श्लोके षष्ठं गुरु ज्ञेयम् सर्वत्र लघु पञ्चमम् । द्विचतुः पादयोर्-र्हस्वं सप्तमं दीर्घमन्ययोः ।।  
5th letter to be Laghu, 6th letter to be Guru. 7th letter to be Laghu in 2nd and 4th quarters and Guru in 1st and 3rd quarters.  
  
Let us check 5th letters. They are पि, तु, **द्धा,** न. There is a non-conformance at **द्धा** in the third quarter.  
6th letters are चा, म(र्ह), च्छ्रे, वि(द्य) - म and वि are Guru, because they are followed by joint letters र्ह and द्य. That is okay.  
7th letters in 1st and 3rd are - वे and यो are Guru. That is okay.  
7th letters in 2nd and 4th are - र्ह and द्य are laghu. That is okay.  
  

By the way, I have committed a mistake in the above analysis. I have always been counting अक्षराणि but calling them as वर्णाः. In this word "वर्णाः" itself, the अक्षराणि are only two व and र्णाः But वर्ण-s are five - व् अ र् ण् आ and also the विसर्ग.

  
I have committed the mistake for so long in so many verses, that I am left wondering how to correct it all over.
