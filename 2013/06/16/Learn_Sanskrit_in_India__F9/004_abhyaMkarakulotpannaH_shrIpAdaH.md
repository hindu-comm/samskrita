Dear Ms./Mr. Artem,  
  
My friend, Mr. Alexander Zachenyuk is in Russia and has learnt Sanskrit on his own, to great depth starting with some Russian book.  
  
I do not know, what level in Sanskrit you have already acquired. I am marking Cc to him, to help set up an interaction between you and him. I think coming to study in India would be good after acquiring some level.  
  
In his introductory message sometime in 2010/11, he had mentioned that his interest was learning the scriptures. His interest was and continues to be literature WITH religious bias. That is exactly opposite of yours. But he is basically selectively studying literature of his interest.  
  
I think you also can pick up such literature as would be of your interest. I would like to recommend Kaadambaree by BaaNabhaTTa as a very interesting prose literature and Meghadootam by Kaalidaasa among poetic literature. Kaadambaree is a novel and Meghadootam is a poem detailing a message, by a distanced lover.  
  
I am translating this message in Simple Sanskrit, which may help you for a check about your level of Sanskrit. Links to my own blogs are also given below.  
  
नमस्ते आर्तेम्-वर्य/वर्ये  
  
मम मित्रं "अलेक्झाण्डर झाषेन्युक्"-वर्यः भवद्देशिकः एवास्ति। तेन संस्कृतं तत्रैव कस्यचित् रशियन्-भाषायां लिखितस्य पुस्तकस्य आधारेण स्वाध्यायेन तथापि अतिसाधु पठितमस्ति ।  
  
न जानामि भवत्संस्कृत-ज्ञानस्य स्तरः कथंविधः अस्ति । संस्कृतं पठितुं भारतं आगमनस्य पूर्वं कस्यचित्स्तरस्य प्राप्तिः उपयोगिनी भवेत् ।  
  
द्वित्रिवर्षपूर्वं "अलेक्झाण्डर झाषेन्युक्"-वर्येण तस्य परिचयात्मके संदेशे तु भणितमासीत् तस्य रुचिः धार्मिकग्रन्थानां पठने एवासीत् । सा तु भवद्रुचेः विरुद्धा एव ! तथापि संस्कृताध्ययनाय यथा स्वीया रुचिः तद्विधानां पुस्तकानां चयनं करणीयं इत्येव ।  
  
भवत्कृते प्रायः बाणभट्टस्य कादंबरीतिगद्यरचना तथा महाकवेः कालिदासस्य मेघदूतमिति काव्यं एते रोचके भवेताम् ।  
मम स्वीयानां जालपुटानां निर्देशाः अपि निम्नं निर्दिष्टाः ।

सस्नेहम्
