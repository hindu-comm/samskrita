If is obligatory for shatr for verbs taking "shap" or "shyan" ending with a; Thus Nityananda Mishra has promperly replied.

  

For the other verbs, in tudAdi, it will take "num" optionally which take "sha". Here, sTu is second class verb and the "shap" is lost after the verbs in this class and does not take "a" ending.
