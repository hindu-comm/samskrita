Another way to memorize dhAtu-pATha: A set of flash-cards have been [published here](https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/387478415)for use with the opensource[anki](http://ankisrs.net/index.html) software, which is available in multiple platforms (Android, iphone, Linux, web, ..) and is very feature rich.

  

Someone ought to similarly publish flash-cards for:

-   ti\~N and sup pratyaya-s for angas and vibhaktis of various types.
