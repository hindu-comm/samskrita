> This is wrong splitting due to modern method of learning. The  
> प्रक्रिया as given by Sri Ramanan is correct. and sandhi software  
> recognizes only the Sandhi rules only and not Samasa prakria, where  
> sandhi is internal with the splitting विग्रहवाक्य of the word. And > the  
> sandhi software doesn't make any difference between अच् प्रत्याहार > in  
> the प्रत्याहार or the its use in the सूत्र-s which is known by  
> commentaries only.  
>   
> The विग्रहवाक्य is न+अच्  
> so, नलोपो नञः, losses the न् in the word न, न = अ+अच्  
> तस्मान्नुडचि -  adds after the नञ्, अच् - i.e. अ in the beginning > of  
> next word, (आदेः परस्य)  
> though "तस्मिन्निति निर्दिष्टे पूर्वस्य" makes it to the previous  
> position, because of अचि, but the "उभयनिर्देशे पञ्चमीनिर्देशो > बलीयान्"  
> makes पञ्चमी stronger than the सप्तमी, Hence, the नुट् goes to the  
> पर, i.e. the अ of the पर "अच्. which makes for form in the process > of  
> नञ्-समास,  
>   
> अ+न्+अ is the split form.  
>   
> When joined, अ+नच् - there is no अन्+अच् in the process. Hence अन् +  
> अच् gives the wrong rule.  
>   
> And even it is joined from the without any order, together, it is  
> wrongly applied, as the न् is not पदान्त, as it is not the > अन्तावयवयव  
> of नञ् which is पूर्वपद as explained and hence as interpreted by > the  
> commentators "ङमो ह्रस्वादचि नित्यम्" does not apply. The Sandhi  
> software doesn't recognize पदान्त, in this case and in many process > of  
> समास.  
> Also it does not recognize whether input is a प्रत्याहार or  
> पाणिनीयसूत्र where it left to the discretion of Panini, as he > applies  
> the same सूत्र ङमो ह्रस्वादचि, - तस्मिन्निति निर्दिष्टे पूर्वस्य, > but  
> it is not applied in इको यणचि etc. If we give यण्+अचि - the software  
> will give यण्णचि and not यणचि, the part of the सूत्र.
