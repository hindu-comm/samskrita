In Mallinatha's commentary on the first sloka of रघुवंशम् , he glosses  
पार्वतीपरमेश्वरौ as शर्वशिवयोः . Is Parvati being referred to as Sarva,  
a name that generally refers to Siva? Or has Mallinatha just flipped  
the order for reasons of समास, and is Parvati being referred to as  
शिवा ?  
  
  

I don't think he has commented पार्वतीपरमेश्वरौ as शर्वशिवयोः which  
is wrong as the gloss to the first in the accusative the gloss in  
genitive case. I could not check the commentary as I do not have  
access right now.  
  
Here is the exact sentence of Mallinatha, which is an independent  
sentence, than a gloss on the text.  
एतेन शर्वशिवयोः सर्वजगज्जनकतया वैशिष्ट्यमिष्टार्थप्रदानशक्तिः  
परमकारुणिकत्वं च सूच्यते  
  
The gloss on पार्वती is given next to this sentence:  
  
पर्वतस्यापत्यं स्त्री पार्वती 'तस्यापत्यम्' इत्यण् ।
