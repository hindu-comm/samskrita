A rough translation is as follows:

  
मा गा इत्यपमङ्गलं व्रजसखे स्नेहेन दीनं वचः।  
तिष्ठेति प्रभुता यथामति चरेत्यैवम् ह्युदासीनता ।  
नो जीवाम विना त्वया यदुपते सम्भाव्यते वा न वा ।  
स्मर्तव्या वयमादरेण भवता यावत् पुनर्दर्शनम् ॥ -  

If we say," Do not go", it will be considered a bad omen; if we say," friend, do go", it will be deemed devoid of love; if we say, "stay back," it will be considered an order; if we say," do as you like," it will be considered disniterestedness; if we say," O,chief of Yadus, we will not live without you" it may not be believed; " Do remeber us with love till we meet again."

  
  
"भो भोः पान्थ कुतो?" "नगरतो" "वार्ता तु काचित् श्रुता?" ।  
"बाढं" "ब्रूहि" "युवा पयोदसमये भार्यां विना जीवति ।"  
"सत्यं जीवति" "जीवतीति कथिता वार्ता मयापि श्रुता "।  
"संकीर्णा पृथिवी जनाश्च बहवः किं तन्न सम्पद्यते" ॥-  
  

"Traveller, from where are you?" "From the city" "Any news?" "Yes" "Tell me" " Young man lives without his wife during the rainy season" "Really?" " I heard so only" " This world has variety; there are many people and what is it that cannot happen?"  
Murthy
