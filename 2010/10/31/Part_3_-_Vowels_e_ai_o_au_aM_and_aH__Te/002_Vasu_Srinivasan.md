> > The tongue should move- flick out towards the lower teeth like in the > consonants La (ल) or Da (ड) but the tongue should neither touch the > palate nor touch the teeth while being spoken. >
> 
> > 
> > The tip of the tongue should start just under the palate, reach out > towards the jaw- turn down just before touching the jaw and then > barely touch the base of the tongue and flick back up. >
