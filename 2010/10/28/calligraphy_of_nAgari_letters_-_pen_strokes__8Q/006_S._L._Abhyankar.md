Dear all,  
  
All these inputs are so magnificent !  
  
A thought comes to mind that there should be some graded reading exercises, which will help a new learner to get to practise lot of reading.  
  
I remember in our Primer of the first standard of Marathi we had such lessons as -  
  
कमल नमन कर। गणपत हरण बघ ।  
मामा चारा घाला । हातात हात धरा । पायात वहाण चढवा ।  
ताटातला चमचा पहा । आरशात पहा । गजाननाला हार घाला ।  
That was easy in Marathi. I guess, it will be more challenging in Sanskrit. I think, as long as one gets practice of READING Devanagari, language can as well be Marathi. Above sentences in Marathi do have meaning and hence make sense also.
